Your shopping bag is empty. Go to the shop Maxeema Biotech – organic/herbal agriculture inputs Home Maxeema Biotech – organic/herbal agriculture inputs Maxeema was established in 2000 by Dr. K.J. Bakshi, chairman and managing director. The company is engaged in the production of organic/herbal agriculture inputs for crop and soil nourishment, and protection. It clocks an annual turnover of Rs. 10 crores. Maxeema is an ISO 2008 certified company with its manufacturing facility near Ahmedabad, Gujarat. The product range is certified as per NPOP standards. [hidepost] The company offers products for soil nourishment (pre/post sowing) and soil pests. The products for plant nourishment are based on seaweed, protein, humates and blend of other proprietary organic substances. It offers products under various segments: • In the nutrition management segment Maxeema offers manures, micronutrients and plant growth promoters • In the seed management segment it offers seed treatment product • In the insect management segment it offers insecticides for mites control, larva control, termite control, mealy bug control, sucking pest control and nematode control • In the disease management segment it offers fungicide and virucide • In the garden management segment it offers garden care products for protection of garden plants and soil • It offers herbal animal feed supplement for milch animals • Maxeema’s all purpose adjuvant product aids efficient applications of organic insecticides, fungicides and weedicides. Its basic functions are: 1. As sticker: makes insecticides stick on leaf surface, resulting low drop off 2. Penetrator: makes insecticide penetrate inside leaf, resulting better results and faster results also  3. Activator: It acts as synergist to many products, resulting in higher efficiency Our Correspondent For more information contact : Dr. K.J.
