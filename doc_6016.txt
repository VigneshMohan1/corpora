Pensioners of Central Government, State Government or any other Government organization can take benefit of this facility. You can register online with the help of this portal and provide your details to get a digital life certificate. Pensioner can find detailed information about this service and locate nearest location of a Jeevan Pramaan Centre. Users can also download mobile apps and avail benefits under this service.
