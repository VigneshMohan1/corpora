Mariappan Bakkiya Nursery Gardens is located at Salem, in Tamil Nadu. Mr. D. Mariappan, a retired forest ranger, is the founder of this government approved nursery which was established 25 years ago. It supplies plants and also educates people on planting and maintenance of their plantations till harvest of the same. The nursery spreads over 1.5 acres and other agriculture land stretches to about 5 acres. It focuses on plants like malabar neem, sandalwood, teak, mahakani, amla, mango, sapota, koiya, and all other tree plants, horticulture, agriculture and ornamental plants. It uses purely organic manure for its plants. However, in unavoidable situations minimum amount of chemicals are used. Its operations are spread over Tamil Nadu and also partly in Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh. Bakkiya Nursery makes its activities known to people through publication in monthly magazines, notice, and stalls in agriculture exhibitions displaying several kinds of plants. “There is lot of demand for malabar neem, sandal plants, teak and red sanders,” shares Mr. Mariappan. Having prior experience in the forest department, Mr. Mariappan chose to run the nursery. “We are from agriculture background. I have a lot of experience in tree plantations in forest department. I have raised tree plantations successfully on more than thousands of hectares in forest department. Personally I have thorough know-how in supplying tree plants in lakhs together to public,” he adds. Mr. Mariappan believes his nursery reflects his abilities of growing plants well. In a nursery, plants need more care and growth thus making it an interesting job for him. With his expertise and experience, he has not yet faced any major challenge or issue in running the nursery. Bakkiya Nursery Gardens is willing to tie-up with companies providing landscape solutions. In future it aims at exporting plants to other states in India and other countries. It is also willing to enter into the field of medicinal plants and herbals.
