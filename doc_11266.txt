Proves with first-time products solar powered sprayer and submersible pump sets M.Nallamuthu Thumba Agro Technologies is located in Dindigul district of Tamil Nadu. It was established in 2003 by Mr. M. Nallamuthu. The company manufactures eco-friendly agricultural equipment that is cost effective in nature. Reliability, durability and efficient performance define its products, according to Mr. Nallamuthu. So far, Thumba Agro Technologies have placed more than 300 permanent sprinkler rain gun systems in India. Other services provided by the company include: • Irrigation controls erection • Lift irrigation pipelines • High pressure motor designing and erection • Advanced valves for multiple solutions • Turnkey irrigation projects • Sprinklers rain gun systems for football, cricket grounds • Dust suppression systems for mines and stone crushers • Micro irrigation like mist, evaporative, and hydroponics • Computerized automatic irrigation systems • Landscape irrigation systems Thumba Agrotechnogies designs its own Thumba dust suppression system for controlling dusts in mines and stone crushers. “Our solar powered sprayer introduced 2 years ago is first of it is kind in the market and is No.1 in the market. Its unbeatable performance requires no fuel, is user friendly, and can be used at homes as UPS,” shares Mr. Nallamuthu. More than 1500 machines have been sold for chaff cutting, vermin composting, and briquetting raw material production all over India. It designs and manufactures special purpose submersible pump sets for raingun sprinklers, domestic purpose and for high pressure lift irrigation pipe lines. Within irrigation, the company’s activities spread to rain gun, sprinkler, mist, and drip irrigation. Its services are extended to dairy and poultry farms. Complete study of a product is done before it is launched into the market. The company aims at providing right and permanent solutions to farmers. “All our products come with very minimum maintenance and long durability of up to 50 years,” Mr. Nallamuthu adds. The company also makes itself available for post-implementation service. All services are provided to farmers irrespective of the scale of their projects. Farmers can also avail subsidies from the government depending on the nature of the project. Thumba Agro Technologies’ mission is to blend past experiences with expectations of the future to bring out technically appropriate and economically feasible irrigation equipment. At present, the company’s R&D wing is keenly working towards introducing many more new irrigation equipment and solar powered machinery. Mr. Nallamuthu holds a diploma in mechanical engineering. His vision since 1995 is to serve the farmer community and to develop possible ways in technologies.
