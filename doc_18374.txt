Travelling to Mysore for a pleasure trip or for a business meeting, one would find a hotel of his/her choice in the city. There are many deluxe hotels as well as resorts in Mysore. Hotel Regaalis is one of the hotels with top-drawer amenities and resplendent ambience. The competent and committed staff of the hotel promises an exceptional stay to its guests. Among the economical hotels also, one will find a wide array of good lodging options for a comfortable stay in the city. It is during the celebrations of Dussehra that the city pulls in tourists from different corners of India and abroad. The lodges and hotels may not be able to offer you a room if you fail to pre-book a room for you and your family. So, do not take a chance if you are visiting the city during the peak season.
