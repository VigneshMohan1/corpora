As per the 2011 Census, Arunachal Pradesh has a total population of 1,382,611; previously it was 1,097,968 in 2001. Out of the total population of Arunachal Pradesh , the population of male are 720,232 and female are 662,379. The total population growth was 25.92 percent and the population of the state forms 0.11 percent of the total population in India. The total area of Arunachal Pradesh is 83,743 sq. km with a Density of 17 per sq km. The Sex Ratio in Arunachal Pradesh is 920 for each 1000 male. In the last census of 2001, the sex ratio was 901 per 1000 males in Arunachal Pradesh. The child sex ratio is 960 per 1000 males and has increased as compared to 798 in the 2001 census. The Literacy rate in Arunachal Pradesh has risen and is 66.95 percent, with male literacy at 73.69 percent and female literacy at 59.57 percent. In figures, total literates in Arunachal Pradesh are 789,943 out of which males are 454,532 and females are 335,411.
