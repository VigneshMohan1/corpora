I’m involved in many activities ranging from social and agricultural improvement in rural India along with members of the UN, affiliations with government agencies in India, planning and implementation of India’s largest high-tech hydroponic undertaking and am retained in many projects internationally, some of which are currently in South Africa, Indonesia,  Australia and Kuwait. I am hired on retainer basis to lead current landscape architecture projects, to consolidate and restructure agricultural and horticultural operations, find hydroponic solutions for agriculture, troubleshooting in all related sectors. In Landscape Architecture / Façade Architecture, I provide client with all drawings, 3D renderings, personalized ecological research, etc. based on project demand. In horticulture / agriculture, I provide solutions with regards of project management as well as solutions in terms of project productivity and cost cutting. In Hydroponics, I provide turn-key solutions in all areas of requirement, including connection of projects to global networks of research and development.
