Life Sciences   FICCI Life Sciences works closely with the government on policy issues, enhancing efficiency, competitiveness and expanding business opportunities for industry through a range of specialized services and global linkages. It also provides a platform for sector specific consensus building and networking. It has been actively involved in organizing conferences and seminars, providing an opportunity for interaction between the industry and the policy makers, to identify the various impediments to the growth and functioning of the industry and to come out with prospective solutions for the same.
