Benefits   Benefit Type Subsidy Eligibility criteria Farmers selling their produce to designated agency How to Avail Application to the Zonal Agriculture Officer of respective Talukas with corresponding sale receipts                   SUPPORT PRICE AND CROP COMPENSATION : To ensure viable agricultural activity the farmers of Goa are provided support price for various crops as under: Sr. No. Crop Pattern A. Support for assured price to agricultural produce 1 Arecanut Rs. 20/- for supari category and Rs. 10/-for other category or the difference between actual price received and Rs. 100/- whichever is lower limited to 6400 kgs per farmer for 4 ha area. 2 Coconut Difference between Rs. 6/- and actual price received limited to Rs. 50,000 nuts per farmer for an area of 10 ha. 3 Cashew Rs. 10/- per kg of cashewnut or the difference between Rs. 50/- and actual price received by the farmer limited to 2000 kg per farmer for an area of 5 ha. 4 Oil Palm Difference between price paid by M/s Godrej IJM Palm Oil Ltd., Khadki Sattari and Rs. 7000/- per tonne of FFB crushed.   B. Additional Support in price for agricultural produce 1 Sugarcane Rs. 500/- ton for sugarcane sold to Sanjeevani Sahakari Sugar Kharkana Ltd. 2 Paddy Rs. 7000/- ton limited to 7,000 kgs per farmer per season or 14,000 kgs per year of the paddy sold to designated agencies. 3 Alsando Rs. 10/- per kg limited to 2,000 kgs per year per farmer sold to Co-operative Society.     Back to Listing This is the official website of Directorate of Agriculture, Government of Goa, India.
