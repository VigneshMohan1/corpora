Objective : To arrange delivery of vastly improved artificial insemination service at the farmers' door step. To bring progressively all breedable females among cattle and buffalo under organized breeding through AI within a period of 10 years. To undertake breed improvement programme for indigenous cattle and buffalo breeds so as to improve their genetic qualities as well as their availability. To provide quality breed inputs in breeding tract of important indigenous breeds so as to prevent the breeds from deterioration and extinction. 2.
