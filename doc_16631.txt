(b)  Communication Skills: Empathise with the farmer Listen actively for content and feeling Exhibit commitment and interest to convince the farmer Avoid defensiveness in conversation. Use personal words in conversation Relate with local experiences, if possible. Avoid use of unnecessary and complex words and sentences. Smooth transition in voice. Speak politely. Close call with greetings. (c)  Computer Skills: Basic knowledge of key board and mouse. Basic knowledge of Internet. Sending and Receiving E-mails.
