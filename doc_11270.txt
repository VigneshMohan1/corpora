Go to the shop TRANSCHEM Agritech – soil and water testing kits Home TRANSCHEM Agritech – soil and water testing kits Vadodara based manufacturers and suppliers of soil and water testing kits Harsh Majmudar TRANSCHEM Agritech was started in 2004 with a basic product, soil test kit for farmers. Since then the company has developed into a company providing complete solutions for soil & water management. It provides soil and water testing kits with in-house developed methods and products. High technology laboratory solutions as well for soil and water analysis are offered by the company. The annual turnover is approximately Rs.15 Crores. The company’s soil testing kit is developed in-house taking into consideration that it can be used by farmers for self-testing. The kit is compact, mobile, user friendly and gives quick results. The kit is designed to evaluate 9 main parameters of soil which are important for understanding soil fertility and fertilizer recommendation. pH, organic carbon, Nitrate nitrogen, ammonical nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, calcium, magnesium, and sulfur are the 9 parameters that the kit evaluates. The Soil testing kit comes with all necessary chemical reagents for testing these minerals and nutrients in the soil. The main purpose of designing the kit was to enable farmers to test their soils themselves. Hence, the steps in instruction manual have been formulated in easy to follow methods. The results are explained through color charts to make evaluation easier. Apart from soil testing kits, TRANSCHEM specializes in water testing kits. These kits test water that is used in irrigation, for drinking and for fisheries and aquaculture. Turnkey projects for complete laboratory set up for soil and water analysis is also taken up by the company. All services right from designing, instrument selection, furniture set up, installation and training is provided by them. These labs can be static lab or mobile vans. Various other products manufactured by the company include Compost testing kits, Arsenic testing kits, and organic foliar spray. The most recent product of the company is a kit that tests effluent water. These kits are bought by retailers, distributors, and NGOs for further supply to farmers in their locality. Some farmers also buy directly from the company. “In case of any issue, confusion or problem in testing, farmers or users can contact our head office. Our technical persons give solutions to online queries. Where there are group of farmers together, demo are also arranged,” shares Mr. Harsh Majmudar, Deputy Manager- Marketing & Exports. TRANSCHEM aims to continue developing products that shall benefit both environment and farming community. “We look to work on new projects and products which have a purpose to satisfy a greener future, environment and a social benefit,” Mr. Majumdar signs off.  Mr. Harsh Majmudar holds a B.E and M.B.A in Marketing. He has 6 years of experience in marketing of chemicals, agriculture equipment and instruments.
