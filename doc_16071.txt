No. Title Implementation of Price Support Scheme (PSS) and Market Intervention Scheme (MIS) through National Agriculture Cooperative Marketing Federation of India (NAFED) Type Non-Plan Scheme Objectives To provide remunerative prices to the farmers by declaring Minimum Support Prices and its implementation through NAFED under PSS and MIS. Salient features The Price Support Scheme (PSS) and Market Intervention Scheme (MIS) ensures remunerative prices to the growers and in case market prices fall below the MSP/MIP, central/state agencies start the purchase operation by paying the MSP/MIP to the farmers. Pattern of Assistance As per the approval announced by the Government at the time of declaration of MSP. Eligibility NAFED and state agencies. Persons to be contacted Joint Secretary (Coop.), DAC, Krishi Bhawan, New Delhi
