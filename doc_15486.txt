Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) of Nagapattinam District is located at Sikkal village 7 km away from Nagapattinam in NH 67 roadways. It is functioning since 01.08.2004 under the administrative control of Tamil Nadu Agricultural University (TNAU) with guidelines of Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) with the major objective of Transfer of Technology in agriculture and allied fields.
