Go to the shop Paks Veterinary Drug Mfg – organic fertilizers Home Paks Veterinary Drug Mfg – organic fertilizers Mumbai based manufacturer and supplier of organic fertilizers Paks Veterinary Drug Mfg is the brain child of Mr. Pradeep Bhatia. It was established in 1999. The annual turnover of the company is Rs. 49 lakhs. Supramino organic fertilizers like amino acid, organic potash, organic zinc, organic calmag, organic nutrical, organic phosphorus are the products that the company offers. Eecogold bioksheera, an animal feed supplement which increases quality and quantity of milk and improves cattle health is another main product of the company. They also export biscuits out of India. The company’s organic products are 100% bio degradable and leave no residues on fruits, vegetables, and cash crops. However annual production of the same is low as farmers are yet to adopt the change. Paks Veterinary is also introducing their products in Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Sultanate of Oman and UAE. The company participates in exhibitions in international markets to advertise their products. Products are also advertised on the internet. “In India the farmers are ignorant of the advantages of organic fertilizers and they spray pesticide very heavily, while in foreign countries they all want now to change to organic fertilizers and save their land and water from toxic pesticides” shares Mr. Bhatia. The only challenge in international market is to get registration in foreign countries. The company plans to continue in the field and develop good environmental friendly products that are safe for humans and animals. Mr. Bhatia is a B.Com graduate with 40 years of experience in this line.
