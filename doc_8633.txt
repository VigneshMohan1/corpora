Statutory Reports and Official Records May 2015 Asian Development Bank–Japan Scholarship Program: Annual Report 2013 The Japan Scholarship Program’s 2013 Annual Report presents the activities of the Asian Development Bank-Japan Scholarship Fund for the year 2013, the 25th year of the fund. May 2015 Japan Fund for Poverty Reduction: Annual Report 2013 The 2013 Annual Report of the Japan Fund for Poverty Reduction (JFPR) presents the background and rationale, implementation progress, and achievements of the JFPR. It is the 13th such report and covers the period 1 January-31 December 2013. May 2015 Clean Energy Financing Partnership Facility: Annual Report 2014 This annual report informs on CEFPF’s operational results and overall implementation progress for the period from 1 January to 31 December 2014. May 2015 Development Effectiveness Review 2014 Report The 2014 Development Effectiveness Review tracks recent development progress in Asia and the Pacific, assesses ADB’s development effectiveness, and identifies areas where ADB’s performance needs to be strengthened. April 2015 2015 Information Statement The Asian Development Bank (ADB) intends to issue its notes and bonds (Securities) from time to time with maturities and on terms determined by market conditions at the time of sale. Information under this information statement, unless otherwise specified, includes data as of 31 December 2014. April 2015 ADB Annual Report 2014 The ADB 2014 Annual Report reviews ADB's operations, projects, internal administration, and financial management. It includes the complete financial statements and opinions of independent auditors. Organizational and operational tables are presented in PDF and Excel formats. April 2015 Strengthening Partnerships: Accountability Mechanism Annual Report 2014 Gaining feedback, learning lessons from past activities and using them imaginatively for charting its future course was the guiding principle for the Accountability Mechanism’s activities during 2014. April 2015 Partnering for Development: Donor Report 2014 ADB is committed to strengthening its partnerships with multilateral and bilateral organizations, foundations, and other emerging development partners to create greater synergies in response to the needs of developing Asia and the Pacific April 2015 ADB and Civil Society Partnership: Annual Report 2013 Showcasing accounts of the value-added contributions of civil society organizations, this publication presents highlights of ADB cooperation with civil society organizations in 2013. April 2015 Project Design Facility—Annual Implementation Status Report (2015) In 2011, the Asian Development Bank (ADB) Board of Directors (the Board) approved the project design facility (PDF) on a pilot basis for an initial 3-year period up to 31 December 2013. The PDF was developed to provide a new complementing financing modality to support project preparation, particularly detailed engineering design. The Board approved an extension of the PDF pilot period, with modifications to the product on 25 February 2014 until 31 December 2017.
