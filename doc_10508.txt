Located at Pune, Maharashtra Abhijit Savant Prerana Laboratories manufacturers Soil Testing Kits and Water Testing Kits at Pune. Mr. Abhijit Savant, Manager talks about soil testing kit with members and answers their question at Webinar – Online Education Forum at www.agricultureinformation.com Since 1985 Prerana Laboratories are manufacturing Soil Testing Kits and it is being used by agriculture farm owners. It is used for testing five major parameters in soil namely (1) Available Nitrogen (N), (2)Available Phosphorus (P), (3)Available Potassium (K), (4)pH and (5)Organic Carbon. It is a complete kit having all the components required for testing the soil for these five basic parameters. With one kit 50 soil tests can be done easily. It also comes with a demo CD and manual providing easy to follow step-by-step instructions. Excerpts: 1 How can it be used after 50 soil test? Once the 50 soil tests are completed the chemicals, testing bottles solutions, colour comparators are exhausted so it has to be taken again which comes in refill kit. The refill kit as the name suggests refills the chemicals and solutions needed for testing the soil. 2 What are the benefits of using soil testing kit? Testing soil for getting good yield in farming is the most crucial. Farmers find in very difficult to get the soil of their farm tested as in their villages or town there are no such laboratories to test the soil. Hence, they have to travel to cities to get the soil tested at any government and private laboratories. Further, they have to again wait for getting the reports. Therefore, to avoid all the stress farmers can use the soil testing kit. The kit enables the farmers to test their farm land soil at the farm itself in an economical way and immediately get the results too. 3 How the soil has to be collected for testing? Soil of the farm has to be collected in scientific way to get the right results. Just picking up the soil and testing shall not provide correct results. The method to collect the soil is clearly explained in the manual and CD that comes with the kit.
