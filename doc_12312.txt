The role of Central Silk Board has always been on high pedestal in the area of human resource development in sericulture. CSB started generating the human resource in India and other countries, by imparting training in sericulture and thus helping in generation of adequate manpower to meet the various challenges faced by the industry. CSB training includes regular structured courses and Entrepreneurship Development and other skill & competence enhancement training programmes in addition to third country training programmes.  The trainees from abroad are financially supported under Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) & Central Silk Board (CSB).   Sitemap Feedback Terms of Use Privacy Policy Copyright © 2015 Central Silk Board.
