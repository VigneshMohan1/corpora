(January 2010) Tabatchnick Fine Foods official logo Tabatchnick is a soup manufacturer based in Somerset, New Jersey . It was founded by Louis Tabatchnick of Newark, New Jersey . He started a chain of restaurants in 1895, followed by the company in 1905.
