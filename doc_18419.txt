It is visited by lakhs of tourists from across the world every year. From a broad variety of cheap hotels, mid-range hotels, luxury hotels and heritage hotels, the visitors can choose the best, as per their budget and needs. While some of the hotels symbolise work of art from the era of the courtly Maharajas, others are cosmopolitan in their appeal. Famous for its heritage hotels, Jaipur hosts numerous picturesque regal residences perched on hilltops. The Oberoi Rajvilas is one of the striking landmarks, which reflects true amalgam of traditional and contemporary style of living. The attribute that distinguishes the hotel from other heritage hotels is its in-house tents set in the fort setting, surrounded with placid green environment. Also, you will find several options to stay amid the bustling city life of Jaipur, in the market area. If one enjoys shopping, then such accommodations can be a good option. One will have an outstanding experience and also can take home the colourful apparels like tie-n-dye and bandhej sarees, accessories, decorative items, handicrafts, etc. with them.
