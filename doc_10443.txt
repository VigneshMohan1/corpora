Go to the shop Mobile Enabled Farm Management Solution Home Mobile Enabled Farm Management Solution Online solution to crop and farm management Mobile Enabled Farm Management provides technology and expertise to create a smarter and safer food supply for consumers around the world. Cloud based technology integrated with mobile apps capture real time data from farms. This platform supports crop planning, crop stage monitoring, farm auditing, harvesting, yield forecasting, real time farm pest resolution along with traceability. Mobile solutions helps agribusinesses to get near real time data from field to the business house and enable them to identify and fix any issues happening at field. Some features of the app include: • Plot & Crop Registration • Auto Geo-Tagging of Plots • Plot area Audit • Agri-Input distribution tracking • Crop Stage monitoring with Visuals • Crop Issue Monitoring with Visuals • Pest and Disease resolution from central location • Harvest Date Re-estimation • Capture Fertilizers and Pesticides Usages • Capture Pick-up Harvest Details and consignment building • Farm registration This technology enables complete transparency in the field and effective management of the field workforce. It helps agri business to manage crops in a project manager defining schedule. Farmers can share past crop, current crop, and detailed farming practices to ensure best practices are shared. CropIn, the company that developed this Mobile Enabled Farm Management provides a wide range of solutions for Agri businesses and other players in the agriculture supply chain. With these solution, crop management can be done from seeding till sowing and executing the entire operation like projects.
