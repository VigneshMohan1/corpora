Hindu devotees as well as saints feel linked, in one way or other, with the grounds of the small holy city because of their belief in the age-old legends that implicate associations of the land with the immortals. Every year millions of people flock to the city to make an endeavour to dip into the holy water of the Ganga River and get sanctified. Living in Varanasi means living in the abode of god, so make sure none of your mistakes demean the levels of your enthusiasm. As far as accommodation is concerned, tourists must prefer to have it within an easy reach from the temples, near the principal arena. The state tourism development authority, keeping in consideration the different economic classes, provides complete information about different types of hotels, lodges as well as ashrams available in the city.
