Based at Haridwar, Uttarakhand Welkin Overseas was established in 2006 in Faridabad. The founders are Mr. Amit Sharma and Mr. Patwal. The company grows and sells button mushroom, milky oyster, agaricus bisporous, calocybe indica, pleutorous and shitake. The annual production of the farm includes 400 tons of button mushroom, 50 tons of milky mushrooms, 50 tons of oyster and 5 tons of shitake. Whole sale vegetable market and exporters are the target customers of the company. Its produce is sold in various states such as Uttarakhand, Delhi, Haryana, Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu and Odissa. Since mushrooms are perishable, the company does not store them. Mr. Sharma believes automation and mechanization are important factors to be considered in processing mushrooms. The company aims to expand and diversify in the future. Both the partners of Welkin Overseas are engineers by qualification.
