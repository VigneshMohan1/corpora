Go to the shop Tirupati Structurals – PVC Pipes, HDP Pipes and irrigation systems Home Tirupati Structurals – PVC Pipes, HDP Pipes and irrigation systems PVC Pipes, HDP Pipes and irrigation systems  manufacturers based at Delhi. Annual turnover Rs.190 crores Tirupati Structurals was established in 1988 by Mr.Lalith Agarwal. Located at Delhi, it clocks approximately annual turnover of 190 Crores. In span of few years company has grown by many folds, starting from base point today it has expanded to two factories in Ghaziabad and another two in Roorkee, Uttar Pradesh. It started manufacturing PVC pipes initially and these days its products line includes HDP pipes, underground pipe line system, drip irrigation and sprinkler system along with water tanks and hand pumps. Mr. Jyoti Kaul is GM Marketing at Tirupati Structurals. Mr. Kaul refers common farmers as the backbone of their company. He says, “most of our product goes to our farmers and some 10%-20% goes to other projects like road project, tunnel project etc.” He further adds, “ we mostly operate in Northern India like Uttar Pradesh and Bihar along with Madhya Pradesh in Central India. Next we plan to expand in Gujarat, Andhra Pradesh and Orissa.” Technology Change Company updates all products with change in technology regularly. Mr. Kaul informs, “ for drip irrigation recently we got a big mould for totally new machine for almost all parts. We have absolutely all automatic machines at our plants precisely at Roorkee.” Up Next So far Tirupati Structurals have completed few export orders to Sri Lanka and African Countries but now it shall focus on exports more aggressively. Within the country, company is keen to have strong presence in Southern India followed by Western States of India.  Mr. Kaul did his B. tech in Agriculture in 1996 and since then he is working with Trupati Structurals.
