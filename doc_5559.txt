Read more» News View All News » Links between migration and land degradation assessed in three West African countries 27 May 2015 Various studies on the links between migration, land degradation and potential innovative financing mechanisms promoting sustainable land management (SLM) have been discussed and validated in West Africa.  During the two-day national consultation workshops, national policy-makers and experts in Burkina Faso, Niger and Senegal contributed to the studies in order to issue guidelines and recommendations for the targeted countries within the context of the Project “West Africa: Promoting Sustainable Land Management (SLM) in migration prone areas through innovative financing mechanisms”. The national consultations took place under the auspices of the Ministries of Environment and Foreign Affairs of the three countries, and were held in Ouagadougou (9-10 April); Niamey (13-14 April) and Dakar (23-24 April), respectively.
