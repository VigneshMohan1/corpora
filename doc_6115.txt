EARN COURSE CREDIT           The Division of Agricultural Sciences and Natural Resources (DASNR) International Programs at Oklahoma State University encourages you to travel to various countries while working on your degree. We offer a wide variety of courses in various countries throughout the world!            DASNR International Programs is the hub of international activities found in DASNR! Students can participate in long-term or short-term study abroad courses throughout the year. We strive to build DASNR students' leadership and coordination skills, international outreach,research and teaching efforts through study abroad programs, international internships and hosting international visitors!          See short and long-term Study Abroad programs tabs to learn more about courses offered and other opportunities available.
