Your shopping bag is empty. Go to the shop Lavanya Mushroom Home Lavanya Mushroom Makers of oyster mushroom at Pune Raju Prakash Gaikwad Lavanya Mushrooms was established in 2011. Mr. Raju Prakash Gaikwad is the founder. Mr. Gaikwad considered mushrooms for cultivation as it doesn’t need lot of water and manpower. The annual turnover amounts to Rs. 10 lakh. Around 1.5 tons of mushrooms are grown at Lavanya Mushrooms every year. The company grows and sells only oyster mushroom as it is contains the most amount of nutrition compared to other mushrooms. Despite being a profitable avenue, the business of oyster mushrooms is yet to be explored by people. All the mushrooms grown by the company are sold to wholesalers and in local markets. Buyers also get in touch with Mr. Gaikwad for direct purchase. Mushrooms are also dried to be sold at later dates. This increases its shelf life, to enable transporting the mushrooms to different locations. Currently, mushrooms are sold in Maharashtra only. However, plans of expanding the business to other states are on the cards. Throwing some light on the challenges faced by mushroom farmers in India, Mr. Gaekwad says, “farmers face problems relating to quality of spawn. Mushrooms are very sensitive to diseases. It is important to maintain the quality of spawn. By using good compost people can get quality mushrooms.” He further shares latest technologies for mushroom processing, collect all kinds of straw and other agro wastes for oyster mushrooms, chop in small pieces, and soak in water for 2-3 hours. Substrate can be pasteurized by hot water (80°C). This is suitable for small scale production Steam pasteurization at 80°C for 2 hours (in autoclave or special steam chambers), suitable for semi-commercial production. Sterilized in an autoclave (after filling in PP bags, plugged with cotton) at 121°C, 15 lb pressure for 15 minutes. This method serves best for commercial production Fill one Kg pasteurized substrate (65% moisture) in Polypropylene bags, plug with non-absorbent cotton. Spawn (Aseptically, @ 5% of wet substrate) while filling in bags in pasteurization method. Spawn run in dark room (24-28°C, 18-30 days variety dependent). Shift to cropping room and make large holes. Pinhead initiation (2-7 days after opening, 13-30°C, variety dependent, humidity 80-85%, light, ventilation) Harvesting (2-4 days after pinhead initiation, variety dependent). Sell as fresh, dry mushroom or as mushroom products. Lavanya Mushrooms plan to supply mushrooms in small packages to make mushrooms affordable for all class of people. Mr. Raju Prakash Gaikwad is a B.Com graduate. He holds two years of experience and immense knowledge in the field of mushrooms.
