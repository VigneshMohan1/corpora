The roadway network of the state of Andhra Pradesh covers an overall span of 91,313 miles or 146,954 km. The state public works department has the responsibility of looking after the roadway transportation system of the state of Andhra Pradesh. The road transportation network of Andhra Pradesh is made up of the following: National Highways (covering 2,949 km or 1,832 miles) State Highways (spanning 42,511 km or 26,415 miles) District Roads (covering 101,484 km or 63,059 miles) The development rate for car proprietorship in the state is the maximum in India and stands at 16 percent.
