These numbers can be of a fire station, police station, water department, electricity department or telecommunication department. You can use the important numbers of Uttarakhand for making any complaint to the above mentioned departments. If you want to complain for some theft or forgery, you need to know the telephone numbers of police station. For asking help from fire department, call the fire station and so on. If you want to complain of a city's administration to the local government, you need to know the telephone numbers of the secretariat.
