here: Home Info Natural Resource Ecology & Management NREM Welcome to Natural Resource Ecology and Management The Department of N atural Resource Ecology and Management includes faculty in fisheries, forestry, rangeland, and wildlife ecology and management that work on projects concerning natural resources that impact Oklahomans. The mission of the NREM Department is to conduct interdisciplinary research, instruction, and extension education to address the sustainable management and conservation of the fishery, forest, rangeland, and wildlife resources of Oklahoma and beyond. Our primary objective is to foster broad understanding of natural resource preservation, conservation, and management through collaboration with related disciplines.
