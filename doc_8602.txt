Transport Urban Development Water Toggle navigation Contact the Economic Research and Regional Cooperation Department The Economic Research and Regional Cooperation Department produces knowledge work on various economic development issues in Asia and the Pacific. The site contains information on the latest ADB flagship publications ( Asian Development Outlook and Key Indicators for Asia and the Pacific ), reports, working papers, and ADB's journal ( Asian Development Review ) . It also provides data and statistics (Statistical Database System) on various economic, social, and financial indicators related to ADB's developing member countries.
