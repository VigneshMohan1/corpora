In future shall setup customer care division to resolve farmers’ farming related queries Om Saravana Trading Co was established in 1975. Mr. Srinivasan K Vasan and his brother manage the company. The annual turnover is about Rs. 5 crores. Based at bangalore, Karnataka. The company deals with jaggery powder, cubes, balls and organic fertilizers. Jaggery items are manufactured by the company’s fellow village land owners based on the quality specifications. It pays personal attention in deciding the quality of the product it gets the orders for, packs as per customer needs,and loads them. The company has its own manufacturing unit of organic fertilizers. All these products are IMO certified. “We take personal care in purchasing the raw material for organic fertilizers. Also for every set or product outcome, we do external lab analysis and confirm the natural benefits that we promise,” shares Mr. Srinivasan. The company also ensures receiving feedback from the end users through a dedicated feedback cell and fixing deficiencies, if any, are reported. No synthetic additive or external chemical compounds to increase the reaction rates are added to the organic fertilizer that the company manufactures. It sticks to the NPK ratio which is the most important part of the Organic Fertilizer. The company also gives personal suggestions to farmers keeping in mind the farmers’ benefits. Mr. Srinivasan believes that this gives the company a cutting edge. The organic fertilizers are supplied mostly to Indian farmers. Despite the prospects of a better turnover with bigger projects internationally, the company believes in giving special attention to farmers. It goes a step ahead and educates farmers about the adverse effect of using chemical fertilizers. The fertilizers are exported to Malaysia, Europe & UAE. Within domestic market, it is supplied in south, east and north regions. Speaking of the pros and cons of exporting agro fertilizers Mr. Srinivasan says, “some local suppliers who add chemical additives to boost the NPK ratio, give the products at a cheaper price which impacts the farmers’ sentiments on product price. Our advantage lies in the awareness that is being created among farmers and other dealers who are actively participating in the Go Organic campaign.” Om Saravana Trading Co wishes to setup a customer care division in the future to spread, teach, and clarify farmer’s doubts on organic fertilizers. Mr. Srinivasan hopes to offer business opportunity of creating organic awareness for young and educated farmers. Mr. Srinivasan is an MCA graduate. He gained knowledge and experience in these fields by interacting with real end users.
