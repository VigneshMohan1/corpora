PUBLICATIONS Drought 2002 - A Report (Part-I & Part-2 (3 Volumes) Drought 2000 Department of Agriculture and Cooperation, Government of India published a report highlighting the gravity of Drought 2002 and the efforts taken by Central Government and State Governments to mitigate the Drought during severe drought during 2002. Also a small book on Drought 2000 highlighting the best practices adopted to counter the drought during that year. Copies of these publications were dispatched to all Central Departments, concerned organizations and major libraries of Agriculture Universities for keeping this Document as a reference to researches and experts.
