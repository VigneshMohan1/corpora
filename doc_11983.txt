Donate Now Our Programmes CARE India, through well formulated and comprehensive programmes, aims to achieve its long term commitments to key population groups.  These programmes on Health, Education, Livelihood, and Disaster Response and Preparedness are innovative in their approach. They involve strategic implementation and constant engagement with other stakeholders to achieve deep and sustainable impact in the lives of the specific populations. To create lasting change, our programmes work towards enhancing the empowerment of women and girls, and alleviating poverty.
