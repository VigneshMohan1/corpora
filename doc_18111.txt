According to the 2011 Census, the total population of Manipur is 2,721,756. Previously, the total population was 2,293,896 in the 2001 Census. The male population is 1,369,764 and the female population is 1,351,992 from the entire population of Manipur. The total population growth since the last decade was 18.65 percent and the population of the state forms 0.22 percent of the entire population in India. Manipur has an area of 22,327 sq. km with a Density of 122 per sq km. The Sex Ratio in Manipur is 987 for each 1000 male and has increased from 978 per 1000 males since the last census. The child sex ratio is 934 per 1000 males and has decreased as compared to 975 in the last census. Manipur has a Literacy rate of 79.85 percent, with male literacy at 86.49 percent and female literacy at 73.17 percent. In figures, the total literates in Manipur are 1,891,196 out of which the males are 1,026,733 and females are 864,463 respectively.
