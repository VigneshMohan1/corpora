People from different corners of the world visit the town to dodge the stresses of busy city life for some time, and rest in the lap of the serene atmosphere of the town. A holiday in Cavelossim for a day or two will be more than a fulfilling experience. So, for a short but a wonderful stay, prefer to book a good accommodation in advance. The visitors can find dozens of lodging options along the beach. From deluxe hotels like five-star hotels like Leela Kempinski Goa Spa Center, Radisson Blu Resort to affordable hotels like Rosarios Inn Guest House which will be fairly easy on the wallet. So, in Cavelossim, you will get multiple options for a comfortable stay worth remembering. Also, some of the hotels offer pleasure-seeking facilities for its visitors like providing bicycles on rent to freely wander around the beautiful town.
