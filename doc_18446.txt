The six Ts- Tourism, Tea, Tiger Hill, Teak, Toy Train, Trekkers' paradise- are considered as the USP of Darjeeling. Domestic as well as international tourists travel to this charming hill station to romance the natural world. From opulent hotels to cheap lodges, the place gives multiple choices of accommodation to the visitors. The nature lovers can choose a hotel perched on the top of a hill to have the best view of the natural delights around you. Central Nirvana is one of the deluxe hotels in Darjeeling which has stood at its place for over a century, and has been recognised for its world class services throughout. Rare blend of modern amenities and historic glory that evokes old English charm lends a unique experience to all its guests. Other luxurious hotels in the region include Mayfair Darjeeling, Cedar Inn and R J Resort. The travellers who are travelling with a shoestring budget can book a cost-effective accommodation. Hotel Snow Hill, Hotel Sunkiss, Fairmont Hotel and Hotel Alice Villa are some of the hotels which fall in the category of budget hotels.
