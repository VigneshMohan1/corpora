DISTRIBUTION RFD Dugdh Jeevan Aadhar- Book Welcome To Delhi Milk Scheme Delhi Milk Scheme is a subordinate office of Ministry of Agriculture, Department of Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries. Its Headquarter and plant are located on the following address:   Delhi Milk Scheme, West Patel Nagar, New Delhi-110008   It was commissioned on Nov 01, 1959 by the then Hon’ble President of India, Dr.Rajendra Prasad with the primary objectives of supplying wholesome milk to the citizen of Delhi at reasonable price as well as to provide remunerative prices to the milk producers. Manufacturing and sale of milk products like Ghee, Table Butter, Yoghurt, Paneer, Chhachh and Flavored Milk is also taken as an allied activity.
