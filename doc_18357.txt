The town is the base camp for the pilgrims to head towards the notable shrine of Shri Mata Vaishno Devi. From the grounds of Katra town, a pilgrim can cover a 13 km long path to reach the shrine on foot, on a palanquin or on a pony. Before starting the pilgrimage, the travellers prefer to have a comfortable accommodation to rest and revive their fervour. The Shri Mata Vaishno Devi Shrine Board (SMVDSB) has opened several lodges, dormitories and ashrams in the town to accommodate the guests visiting the town. These accommodations can be availed at free of cost or one can opt for shrine's rented accommodation. Apart form these, one will find broad and varied private lodging options in the town. Pilgrims can also pre-book a room for themselves, which must be done by the travellers to avoid any inconvenience, especially during the peak season. For the ease of the devotees, certain hotels have initiated an additional service of providing Yatra slip for the holy centre at free of cost at their receptions.
