Go to the shop Organic India Home Organic India To attain mission for promoting true wellness for mankind, produces and supplies pure organic products Dr. Amit Kumar Organic India is an internationally certified organic health & wellness products manufacturing and marketing company. It was established by Mr. Yo.Lev in 1997. To offer world class authentic products to everyone in the chain, from farmers and employees to end customers, was the driving force that led to its establishment. The annual turnover of the company is Rs. 150 crores. Its operations are spread over 40 countries. Flagship brand Tulsi Tea with 23 different varieties, 26 health supplements with Organic Ghee and Chywanprash are the main products of the company. These products are available both online and in exclusive Organic India stores. “We have a unique business model in which Organic India promotes true wellness in favor of mankind with a new hope in mind as mission of pure authentic products,” shares Dr. Amit Kumar, Marketing Head of the company. The company’s organic health products meet the highest international quality standards. Organic products and medicines offering proven benefits are backed by clinical research. “Our products are pure, effective, and safe for consumption,” Dr. Amit further adds. Raw materials used in the products are procured from their own farmers at marginal price structure. 20,000 farmers are associated with the company directly and indirectly. Processing plants are located in Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh and Andhra Pradesh. Organic India conducts an observation on organic farming for 3 years by the farmers who wish to get associated with the company. Standards like heavy metal limitation, no chemically treated seeds, no synthetic fumigant, and no chemical pesticide are observed. The company ensures meeting its farmers twice a year to educate them on its products and their benefits. Exclusive Organic India stores across the country provide free health checkup and free consultation to create awareness of the company’s products. The company also advertises its products through print media and online forums. Live campaign to educate people in societies and parks, meetings with doctors are also undertaken. Organic India has been felicitated with various awards such as APEA award 2009 for Dynamic Young Entrepreneur and Best Excise Award. “The company is committed to setting new standard of quality to overcome globally recognized as reliable and competitive supplier of health products,” Mr. Kumar adds. The company aims to increase its annual turnover to Rs. 250 crores in the coming year. Dr. Amit Kumar holds a B.Pharma, with MBA and MD (Alternative Medicine).
