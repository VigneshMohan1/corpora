and Public Distribution is responsible for management of the food economy of the nation. It undertakes various activities, such as procurement of food items, their storage, movement and delivery to the distributing agencies. This section provides requisite information pertaining to the Central, State Government Departments and various other institutions handling the public distribution system. Information related to the commodities, consumer affairs, consumer cooperatives and schemes is also available in this section.
