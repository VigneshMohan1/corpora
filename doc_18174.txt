However Compare Infobase Limited and its directors do not own any responsibility for the correctness or authenticity of the same. India has both public and private universities. The public universities are supported by the Indian government and the state governments. The private universities are generally supported by different organization, groups and societies. In India, the universities are recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC), under the control of the University Grants Commission Act, 1956. Fifteen additional professional councils are also established which help control the different facets of accreditation and coordination. There are four types of universities in India, they are; Central universities, State universities, Deemed universities and Private universities.
