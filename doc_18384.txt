Organised agricultural activities of the Britishers have today made Wayanad the melting pot of Indian spices, tea, coffee and cocoa. Blessed at a height of 2100 m above sea level, its uber-cool climes create a perfect dwelling place for a variety of animal and plant species. Tourists visit Wayanad to get a firsthand experience of the beauteous intrigue created by luminous mountains, dense forests, plateaus and deep valleys. Wayanad dishes out several hotels that are not only affordable but also service oriented. These hotels exude an ambience of modern necessities interspersed with traditional hospitality and decor. Well furnished rooms equipped with basic requirements like audio visual facilities, telephone, television, private bathroom, wireless internet connection etc. are easily available. Wayanad hotels are economical, yet tourists are not deprived of recreational facilities like spa, swimming pool, games room etc. Some major Wayanad hotels are Karapuzha Village Resort, Sunbird Garden Resort, Hotel Great Jubilee, Vythiri Meadows, Vythiri Village, Upavan Resort, Cheeral Green Homestay, Hotel Brahmagiri etc. The plush interiors together with very attentive room service of these hotels nestled in the eco friendly surroundings of Wayanad are bound to make you feel over the top.
