Posted in About Finer Twitter To bring Trade, Industry & Commerce of the region under one umbrella Enhance the flow of investments into Trade, Industry and Infrastructure of the region Generate additional employment opportunities in the region Identify and deploy the State resources of the region for productive use Special focus on promoting the traditional industries through the cluster approach with private sector participation with a view to ensure the economic development of the remote and hill areas of the North Eastern States Pursue the agenda of Infrastructure development, enhance private sector participation, human resource development, expansion of marketing linkages and above all protection of the environment is prime objective To identify thrust areas, in particular industries based on locally available raw materials and skills and draw up integrated development plans for each such area Facilitate in raising the per capita income levels and standards of living of the people Promote the region to bring it to the National Industrial Map Help the SME for developing linkages for the global market Create awareness for “Quality” of products and services Ensure equitable industrial development in all regions of North East and putting it on a high growth path Guide the Governments to encourage private sector participation including attracting foreign and Non Resident Indian investment. Build awareness for environment friendly or green industries. Facilitate programs for capacity building and skill development Advocate reforms in Banking and Financial Sector to ease the lending and credit flow for the SMEs Above all, create an Industry friendly environment with all the State Government of North East playing the role of a facilitator in industrial development.
