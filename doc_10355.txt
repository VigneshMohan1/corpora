Go to the shop Filbert Industries – Green houses, shade houses Home Filbert Industries – Green houses, shade houses Filbert Industries is located in Nagpur in Maharashtra state. The proprietor of the firm is Mr. Rajeev Jagtap. The firm deals in Green houses, shade houses, supply of planting materials, irrigation and fogging systems, naturally ventilated multi span and shadenet business. Nowadays, protected cultivation is becoming popular, because of various conditions like climate variation and other reasons. Huge investment is needed in installing Green houses and shadenets which only a few can afford. Filbert’s Green houses are sturdy enough to withstand heavy winds and match to the specific requirements of every crop and climatic conditions. It allows maximum ventilation and thus mitigate against severe hot and dry weather. Green houses are ideal for cut flowers like Roses, Gerbera, Carnation, Anthurium, Lillium etc., vegetables like coloured Capsicum, Broccoli, Zukini, Bitter Gourd, Cherry Tomato, Cucumber etc shares Mr. Jagtap. Filbert Industries proprietor Mr. Rajeev Jagtap says, “No new technology has come up for Green houses and Shadenets. We are using the prevailing one only. Day by day response from farmers are increasing and public are becoming more aware and as a result they come forward to accept the Shadenet and Green houses. We are solely dependent on mouth publicity for our products. Anybody who wants to invest in this, goes and see the project we have erected earlier, talks to the concerned person, gets complete information about their experience and other details and then they approaches us for erecting the same. There are lots of people who are techno savvy, who by using internet, also find out the details. I also help those who are staying in rural areas with no internet facility,’’ adds Rajeev.
