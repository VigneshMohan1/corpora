Go to the shop AOS Products – Essential oil manufacturers Home AOS Products – Essential oil manufacturers Essential oil manufacturers and suppliers at Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh “It is really difficult to find natural products nowadays, and so the main aim was to provide natural products,” shares Mr. Abhilash Kumar Pandey, Managing Director as what lead him and Mr. Sandeep Maheshwari to establish AOS Products in the year 2009. The company focus to provide high quality natural products at good price. The products are sold under the brand name AOS Products. The product range includes mint products, essential oils, aromatherapy oils, spice and oleoresins, pine derivatives and carrier oils. “The most popular product are mainly the menthol, peppermint oil, spearmint oil, eucalyptus oil, terpin hydrtae, lemongrass, jojoba oi, soybean oil, arachis oil,” shares Mr. Pandey. Of these, the featured products are menthol and terpin hydrate. “We can say so because our menthol and terpin hydrate is of extra pure quality. Our menthol has 99.9% of L-menthol,” he shares. These products are required in huge quantities in various industries, mainly in pharmaceutical and personal care. The usage of extra pure quality reduces the quantity consumption thus helping the manufacturer to reduce the cost of their product without any compromise in quality. “All the products after manufacturing are checked on physical and chemical parameters. We have our own in-house lab,” clarifies Mr Pandey, “The oils are packed in export worthy packing in various quantities as required by customer.” The company procures the raw material mainly from farmers. They follow the GMP quality measurement guidelines while procuring the raw materials. For extraction of various oils the company makes use of various available technologies such as cold pressed, expeller method, solvent extraction method etc. The company made an initial investment of Rs 2lacs. The quantum of products sold in the year 2014-2015 in both domestic and international market amounted to 150 MT worth about Rs 12 crore. “We are dealing in Pan India and in more than 60 countries around the globe,” shares Mr Pandey. At present there are 5 employees working in the lab. “We wanted to take nature in the purest form to the people,” says Mr. Pandey hence the company is into various aromatic and essential oils. These oils have various therapeutic and medicinal qualities and hence they are used in various medicine, aromatherapy, and skin care products etc. “The biggest achievement that we can say is we have gained trust of our customer,” shares Mr Pandey happily. The company plans to expand on the production front. “We are re-investing in our business and we are in expansion phase,” he adds. Mr. Abhilash Kumar Pandey holds an MBA and has 18+ years industry experience.
