Opinion Poll on Road Safety by Ministry of Road Transport and Highways Rate it: Select rating Poor Okay Good Great Awesome   Submit your opinions on road safety specifically for identification of black spots (accident prone area) with Ministry of Road Transport and Highways. Users can share their opinion related to road safety in their locality by filling an online form. Consultation papers by Telecom Regulatory Authority of India Rate it: Select rating Poor Okay Good Great Awesome   Users can give their consultation for various policies, regulations, amendments, market research, guidelines, etc. of Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI). Details related to consultation papers by TRAI, their date of release, last date of submitting comments, divisions of TRAI, etc. are also available. Public consultation on Electronics, IT and Telecom Policies 2011 Rate it: Select rating Poor Okay Good Great Awesome   Give your consultation on Electronics, IT and Telecom Policies 2011. Details of the Triad of Policies to drive a National Agenda for ICTE, National Policy on Electronics 2011, National Policy on Information Technology-2011 and National Telecom Policy-2011 are available. Users can send their opinion/suggestion directly in the given email addresses.
