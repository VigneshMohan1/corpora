[INDIA] 226022 Ray Welfare Society is pleased to present this proposal for your review. We look forward to propose you to provide computer literacy program for the people of rural areas. Ray Welfare Society already working in the area of computer training by associating with SRAI SAHAJ, a joint body of Uttar Pradesh Government and CMC (a subsidiary of TATA Industries.). The objective of the Computer Literacy Program is to help all students with lack of resources living in rural areas to enables them in basic computer skills to higher level computer courses by this they can able to survive and earn better place in society.
