Add Thread to del.icio.us Bookmark in Technorati Tweet this thread Thread Tools Show Printable Version Subscribe to this Thread… 7 Hours Ago #1 rachel123 Join Date May 2015 Posts 10 Technology Notes of biomass pellet mill As the important equipment in biomass energy, Fote biomass pellet mill is used to pelletize all kinds of wood chips and sawdust. The biomass pellet mill need to process crude fiber materials, which is featured with low density, poor adhesion, difficult molding, low yields, large power consumption and high pressure demand. So, in your production process, you need know some technology notes. Fote Heavy machinery has many matured production technology and our biomass pellet mill has attracted many foreign customers. So, we have rich experience in the sales of biomass pellet machine, and you can trust our production quality. In accordance the experience from production and operation, our technist induct the maintenance methods to following sections. We hope that our maintenance methods will promote smoothly operation of the industry. Change the accessories of the biomass pellet machine in daily operation. Work intensity of the biomass pellet mill is larger than traditional pellet mill. So, you should keep tracking and monitoring during the production process, such as current, voltage, noise, dust and particle size. Then you can make appropriate adjustments in accordance with production conditions. Changing the failure equipments to make sure the continuously production of the pellet machine. Lubrication and cooling of the biomass pellet machine are two important functions. The rollers can push the sawdust into the inner hole by extrusion. After long time operation, the biomass pellet mill will be hot. So, timely oiling and cooling can realize continuously using of the biomass pellet mill. In your operation, you can realize continuously production by high quality machine and daily maintenance. Fote has first-class technical team, and we can provide you personalized design in accordance with your real situation.
