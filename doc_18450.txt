To suit the pockets of all kinds of visitors, the Cultural Capital of India offers numerous lodging options. A stay in Kolkata must give you a taste of its cultural and historical riches. The hotels built in old style of architecture lend you a glimpse of the vibrant lifestyle of the city. Also, you can live in one of the structures created and designed by the Englishmen during the colonial rule, or you can opt for an accommodation with modern infrastructure. Whether you have selected a regal resort or a budget hotel, your stay in Kolkata is going to be very smooth because of the dedicated staff of the hotels. Most of the hotels in the city provide 24*7 services for their customers. Apart from this, the visitors have an option to place special requests in case one requires something specifically. Most of the deluxe hotels make you fill a "Preferences" form at the time of booking which includes a "Special or Accessibility Requests" column. A distinctive requirement asked by some of the hotels in Kolkata at the time of booking is a proof of marriage, if you want a room as a couple. So, before you head towards one of the most populous cities of the country, make sure that you have enough sources of information to avoid any inconvenience during your stay. However, it is advisable not to involve any tout or travel agent for the purpose of booking hotels.
