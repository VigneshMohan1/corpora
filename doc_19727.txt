Management Info Oklahoma Cooperative Extension Service Gardening, Insects & Pest Management The Oklahoma Cooperative Extension Service develops programs based on science-based, objective information to help Oklahomans solve problems, promote leadership, and manage resources wisely. Below are websites and documents that provide information about Gardening, Insects & Pest Management. Fact Sheets The Oklahoma Cooperative Extension Service creates Fact Sheets to provide research based information on a variety of topics.  Specific topics in the gardening, insects & pest management section include: lawn & garden care, insects & diseases, pesticides and plant diseases. Click on the links below to view Fact Sheets relating to these topics. Lawn, Yard & Garden Insects Websites Gardening Information for Fruit, Vegetable and Turf Growers and Commercial Producers - A collection of information regarding commercial horticulture and fruit, vegetable and turf production. Lawn & Garden Resources Master Gardener Program - The Master Gardener Program is a volunteer training program designed to help county extension centers meet the demands for consumer horticulture information. Oklahoma Invasive Species - The Oklahoma Invasive Species Web site lists non-native species affecting Oklahoma and gives information about how to control them. OK Plant ID - OK Plant ID is a database that helps users to identify common Oklahoma plants. OK Plant Trees - OK Plant Trees provides resources for community forestry in the state of Oklahoma. Oklahoma Gardening TV Show - For the last 33 years Oklahoma Gardening has endeavored to provide the best research-based gardening and horticulture information available on television. Oklahoma Turf Grass Extension Program OSU Botanic Garden Teach Plants - Teach Plants helps students and educators with the identification of different plant types at different stages of growth. Superior Plants Recommended for Oklahoma Weed Science Extension Program Insects Africanized Honey Bees - Information on the incidence of Africanized Honey Bees in Oklahoma. Mosquitoes & West Nile Virus - Information on the incidence of Mosqitoes and West Nile Virus in Oklahoma Red Imported Fire Ants - Information on the incidence of Red Fire Ants in Oklahoma Pest Management Integrated Pest Management - The Oklahoma Integrated Pest Management (IPM) program is a diverse, highly interdisciplinary program designed to be a first-line resource for IPM information to the rural and urban communities of Oklahoma. Pesticide Safety Education Program Pinkston Education Facility for Structural and Urban Pest Control - The Pinkston Education Facility seeks to present both standard and unique construction methods and materials typically encountered during the application of a termite control program. Plant Disease & Insects Diagnostic Laboratory - The Plant Disease & Insects Diagnostic Laboratory provides residents in the state of Oklahoma with both accurate diagnoses of plant diseases and insect pests and recommendations for their control.
