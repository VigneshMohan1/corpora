But even where the goals have been met, millions of people are still deprived, says a new United Nations report. 28 May 2015 ADB Prices $2 billion Global Benchmark Bond ADB returned to the US dollar bond market yesterday with the pricing of a $2 billion 3-year global benchmark bond issue. Proceeds will be part of ADB’s ordinary capital resources and used in its non-concessional operations. 26 May 2015 ADB-KDI Forum Urges Greater Regional Cooperation for Financial Stability Asia and the Pacific needs to make greater efforts to promote financial sector reform and strengthen regional cooperation to safeguard the region’s economies against future financial shocks.  25 May 2015 BAKIDA KEÇİRİLƏCƏK REGİONAL TƏHSİL KONFRANSDA AZƏRBAYCANIN 12-İLLİK ORTA TƏHSİL SİSTEMİNƏ KEÇİDİ MÜZAKİRƏ EDİLƏCƏK Mərkəzi və Qərbi Asiyadan olan təhsil üzrə beynəlxalq ekspertlər 2 günlük regional konfransda iştirak etmək üçün Bakıya toplaşacaqlar. 25 May 2015 Rebuilding Livelihoods, Building Back Better Key for Nepal, Says ADB Vice-President Nepal needs immediate help to rebuild livelihoods after last month’s devastating earthquake but in the longer term, the country must make sure it is more resilient to future disasters, said ADB Vice-President Bindu N.
