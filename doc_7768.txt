Resources Training Opportunities Search Advanced Search… Login Info DASNR Technology Services and Support The purpose of this website is to enhance the information technology (IT) training and support services we provide to DASNR Administration, Administrative Support Units, District Extension Directors, Area Extension Specialists, Extension Educators, and District/Area/County Support Staff.  For our internal (DASNR) clientele we have included a number of resources here to help you resolve technology-related problems. In addition to sessions in a group or office setting, we offer a monthly online training program titled Cooperative Conversations .  Along with contact information, a feedback form is available on our Contact Us page.   Document Actions Send this Print this Spotlight on DASNR IT I posted to Facebook and you would never guess what happened next.
