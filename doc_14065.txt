Posted in About Finer Twitter The Chamber has a permanent Secretariat headed by the Executive Secretary who is assisted by support staff. The Secretariat works under the guidance and supervision of the Elected Governing Board for a period of two years. By constitution it has 15 elected directors who in turn elect / select their chairman. Apart from this the chairman can nominate 5 more directors with the approval of the board.
