SERVICES Management Development Programmes In-house Training programmes for companies Management Development Programmes This Centre organises short duration management development programmes for the middle and senior level executives of Indian industries covering manufacturing sector as also in the areas of Developing People Potential Finance, Taxation, Risk Management Marketing & Sales Operations Management Supply Chain, etc. In a year approximately 80 - 90 programmes are held. The uniqueness of this Centre is that it promotes learning through small groups and intensive interaction with the faculty, which is predominantly drawn from industry experts and practitioners. In-company Programmes The Centre offers customised company specific programmes also. Such programmes have been held so far all over the country for a large number of companies. Programme for Visionary Corporate Leaders in Manufacturing : VLFM VLFM is a national programme to develop leaders in manufacturing under the Indo-Japan Cooperation Agreement 2006 which is held in India under the guidance of Prof Shoji Shiba. Confederation of Indian Industry is implementing the programme with support from Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) and National Manufacturing Competitiveness Council (NMCC). A unique programme that brings together senior leaders from the manufacturing sector and urges them to look beyond the obvious, to see the invisible and to unearth the latent needs of the customer. It focuses on imparting skill-based training in addition to conventional methods of knowledge and understanding. Learningï¿½s gained from the programme have immediate application in the industry. The programme comprises five modules of five days each through 30% theory and 70% practical application. Between each module there is be a five to seven weeks gap. During this period, in addition to the homework, participants are expected to apply their learningï¿½s at their workplace.
