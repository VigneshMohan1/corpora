SJDP Area of operation and coverage under the programme 1. The programme will be implemented in most of the major jute and allied fibre growing states. The programme will cover jute and allied fibre crops, such as jute, mesta, sunnhemp, ramie, sisal. The IJDP will be implemented in the selected districts. It has been decided to cover almost the entire area of the selected districts under the IJDP. However, for ramie the programme will be implemented in some selected districts to be decided by the State Governments and only in a concentrated manner within the district. 2 The programme on jute will be implemented in the states of Assam , Bihar, Meghalaya, Orissa, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal . 3 The mesta programme will be implemented in the states of Andhra Pradesh, Bihar , Meghalaya , Orissa and Tripura.  4 The programme of sunnhemp will be implemented in the State of Uttar Pradesh . The development programme will be implemented in selected districts having concentrated area in the identified states. 5 The ramie programme will be implemented in five states, namely, Arunachal Pradesh , Assam , Meghalaya, Nagaland and West Bengal .  6 The Department will have the flexibility to include or exclude States/Districts/Areas in the Mini Mission-II depending on the ground situation in consultation with States/UTs.                                                               7 The state-wise districts and area to be covered under the development programme are as under    State District Target of Coverage (‘000 ha) Jute Mesta Sunn- hemp Total Andhra Pradesh Srikakulam and Vizianagaram (2 districts) -
