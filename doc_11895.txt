MolScript: The Official Website MolScript: The Official Website MolScript is a program for displaying molecular 3D structures, such as proteins, in both schematic and detailed representations. Output formats: PostScript, Raster3D, VRML 2.0, interactive OpenGL, SGI (aka RGB), JPEG, EPS, PNG, GIF. Current version: 2.1.2 (released 12 Jan 1999) P. J. Kraulis, Journal of Applied Crystallography (1991) v 24, pp 946-950. J. Appl. Cryst. website MolScript is Open Source as of 1 Nov 2014, and available at GitHub .
