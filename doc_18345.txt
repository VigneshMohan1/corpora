The town is also popular as 'the Valley of the Gods' or 'Dev Bhumi' because of the numerous temples present in and around the valley. The traditional charm of the place has sustained over the years and can be felt during your stay in Kullu. There are not many lodging options available in the town. However, among the ones present, one can find budget as well as deluxe hotels and resorts. Also, the visitors have an alternative of spending their vacations in a tree house cottage, built in perfect natural setting. Visitors in huge number throng to the valley during the festival of Dussehra and Holi, to taste the true blend of culture, religion and colours. Tourists from different places visit Kullu and participate in the grand celebration at the Hindu temples of the valley.
