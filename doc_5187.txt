Without proper rendering support , you may see question marks, boxes, or other symbols instead of Khmer script . Kampot ( Khmer : ខេត្តកំពត IPA:  [kɑmpɔːt] ) is a province ( khaet ) of Cambodia . It borders the provinces of Koh Kong and Kampong Speu to the north, Takeo and Kep to the east and Sihanoukville to the west. To its south it has a coastline of around 45 km on the Gulf of Thailand . Its capital is Kampot town. Kampot has a population of 627,884 [ 2 ] and consist of eight districts divided into 92 communes with a total of 477 villages. [ 3 ] Touk Meas City is located in Kampot Province.
