About the Company Incorporated in the year 1960 Main raw material used was Bamboo Megre allotment of bamboo by government necessitated our shifting to use of hard wood Started using bagasse besides wood since 1984 SPB is the first industry to use bagasse as rawmaterial for paper making. Govt. of India also supported the industrial move of using bagasse for paper making by giving excise concession. Under the leadership of SPB Asia's biggest Paper Mill 'TNPL' was installed using bagasse as the main raw material. Ponni Sugars and Chemicals was also installed in the near vicinity to cater to the bagasse demand of SPB. 2. Production Annual production:   1,20,000 M.T or 350 M.T per day 3.
