Leo Daniel Amalraj Mr. E. Leo Daniel Amalraj works as a technical consultant with Varsha Biscience and Technology India Pvt. Ltd. He holds Ph.D in Biotechnology and 11 years of experience in bio-fertilizers and bio-pesticide Industry. Mr. Leo has been offering his services to Varsha Biscience since two years. As a consultant Mr. Leo offers services in manufacturing process, marketing strategies, acquiring license from respective authorities, QC protocol and CIB registration procedures. He also contributes to lab designing, infrastructure erection, statutory licenses, production and QC protocols, GLP, packet design, leat and label contents, and bio-efficacy evaluation. He extends his services in devising strategy provided the entrepreneur takes care of marketing. Mr. Leo admits marketing is the biggest challenge in manufacturing and selling bio-fertilizer and bio-pesticides. Speaking of his recommendation on bio-fertilizer and bio-pesticides Mr. Leo says “There are 6 bio-fertilizers included in Fertilizer control order (FCO) 1985. They are PSB, Azospirillum, Azotobacter, Rhizobium, Potash mobilizer and Zinc solubilizing bacteria, whereas for bio-pesticides, they are classified under pesticide act.” “Low cost investment, training on hands on experience to handle microbes etc. are the special features of our services,” shares Mr. Leo. His clients include small scale entrepreneurs.
