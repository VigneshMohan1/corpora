Your shopping bag is empty. Go to the shop Waman Enterprises – natural ventilator polyhouse Home Waman Enterprises – natural ventilator polyhouse Waman Enterprises was established in 1986. It is located in Pune, Maharashtra state. Mr. Ajay Narkhede is the proprietor of the firm. The firm manufactures natural ventilator polyhouse, polyhouse, and net-houses, round-type and flat-type. The firm also manufactures greenhouses for individual farmers and for Universities for their research purpose. Apart from greenhouses, the firm markets seed processing machineries for grain industries. The firm’s annual turnover is around Rs. 1 crore. To create awareness about their products, Waman Enterprises has got its own website and is registered with IndiaMART. The main publicity is through word of mouth. To provide greenhouses with advanced technology, the firm is working towards automation of various segments like curtain opening, roof sliding, etc. If a farmer invests a higher amount, the firm is planning to provide air-circulator fans too. Now the firm’s main thrust is on modern technology. The firm’s target customers are progressive farmers from Maharashtra, seed industries, Indian Council of Agricultural Research Department (ICAR), Agriculture Universities, etc. The firm’s main market is Maharashtra state.  Mr. Ajay Narkhede is a chemical engineer in Pulp and Paper Technology. He has got 20 years of experience in agriculture field. Mr. Narkhede says, “80 percent of farmers are using greenhouses for flower cultivation. Only 20 percent are using for vegetable cultivation. Roses, Gerbera and Carnation – these flowers are grown mainly and the farmers are getting good price in the market.’’ “Greenhouses are the need of the time, and since there is uncertainty in the nature, this technology will get a boost,’’ adds Mr. Ajay Narkhede.
