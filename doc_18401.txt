Located in the Rahata Tehsil in Ahmednagar, Maharashtra, the small town is visited by more than 25,000 devotees everyday. The tourism industry has flourished in the town over the years, yet the holy town has been kept in pristine condition through the generosity of believers. A feature common to almost all the hotels in Shirdi is the calm and serene atmosphere to render pure relaxation to the visitors. To complement the tranquil environment of the chief shrine, there are multiple ashrams in the region with vast spreads of lush green lawns. One such accommodation is the Sai Ashram (Phase 1) with a capacity to accommodate about 9000 devotees. In total, there are 1536 rooms, of which 1152 are common rooms, while 384 are AC rooms. New Bhakta Niwas and Dwarawati are the other alternatives available with similar facilities at affordable prices. However, if money is not an obstacle then one can go for a hotel providing all the facilities. Within walking distance from the temple, devotees can locate hotels as well as resorts, ranging from economical class to deluxe hotels.
