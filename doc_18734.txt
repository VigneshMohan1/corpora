New Delhi Railway Station is among the important railheads of the Northern Railways. The New Delhi Railway Station (NDLS) is the main rail terminus in New Delhi. New Delhi Station counts as the 2nd busiest and among the largest in the country. More than 300 trains ply from this railway station in New Delhi, every day, from 16 platforms. The New Delhi station holds the record for the largest route interlocking system in the world. The New Delhi railway station is located at a distance of about about 2 kms from the Connaught Place, Central Delhi , 19 kms from Ghaziabad and Uttarpradesh , 31 kms from Gurgaon and Haryana . You can avail computerized reservation services at the railway station of New Delhi. The rail terminus is also replete with tourist information cell. Indira Gandhi International Airport is the nearest airport to the New Delhi Railway Terminus.
