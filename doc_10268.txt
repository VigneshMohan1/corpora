Located at Siliguri, West Bengal Ashram Bio health is a sister concern of Export Agency established in 1999. The company was established in 2007-08. Mr. Mr Ratanlal Agarwal is the founder of the company. Its turnover ranges from Rs. 8 to 10 crores. The company has been involved in contract farming since 2006. Twenty seven self-help groups are associated with the company to grow high value medicinal plants. Such farming has brought about a change in in lives of farmers growing medicinal plants. Mr. R.K.Sinhal explains, “farmers are growing high value medicinal plants which are bringing about an improvement in their economic conditions through high prices of the plants. Plots in hilly areas have been seeing a tremendous change.” The contract farmers of the company focus on both organic and inorganic farming, since technical requirement is tough. The prices in contract farming of medicinal plants are generally fixed. Price variations do not occur as per the market. While this may pose as a challenge to the company, it benefits the farmers. Farmers can concentrate on the yield of quality and try to make as much production as possible. They need not worry about sale of products, and can adopt modern techniques to get more benefits. They are thus ensured of the security of their produce. “Contract farming is one of best solution to develop and get economy scale to higher for the farmers specially. However, it will take more years to understand its value and to get connected with it”, adds Mr. Sinhal. Representatives of Ashrams Bio Health visit remote areas and educate farmers about contract farming. It also conducts seminars and official meetings. Farmers willing to associate themselves with the company may get in touch with such representatives or contact the company through phone. The company plans to expand contract farming in the future. Its first target is to cover up the entire state of West Bengal, which holds a very large area in farming. It then plans to expand the activity to other cities.
