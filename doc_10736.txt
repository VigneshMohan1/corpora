HOW TO USE – “KANG KANG” LIQUID SEED TREATMENT – COATING For 1 Kg Seeds use 5-6ml. of KANG KANG Liquid mixed with just enough quantity of water to have a thick coating. Note:  For 1.5 Kg Seeds of BT COTTON, use 100ml. of KANG KANG Liquid without mixing with Water. ROOT DIP / TRANSPLANTING (As in Paddy) Take 5 ml. of KANG KANG Liquid per litre of water. FOLIAR SPRAY: (To Be Done in 3 Stages) Use 2-4ml. of KANG KANG Liquid / Litre of water: Stage 1: At the seed germination stage. Stage 2: At the flowering stage. Stage 3: 25-30 days before harvesting of the crop. DRIP & SPRINKLER IRRIGATION: (To Be Done in 3 Stages) Stage 1: 150-200ml. of KANG KANG Liquid / Acre at the seed germination Stage 2: 400-500ml. of KANG KANG Liquid / Acre at the flowering stage.
