Each year, students from academies across the country attend the event to showcase their work, practice their networking skills, and meet role models from the business community.   NAF will honor Cisco Systems and KPMG at the 2015 Benefit, Wednesday, June 10 at Mandarin Oriental Hotel in New York City.
