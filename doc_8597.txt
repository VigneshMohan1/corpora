ADB works with each developing member country to map out a medium-term development strategy and a 3-year country operations business plan (COBP) to implement it. The regional cooperation strategy (RCS) outlines how an ADB-defined region or subregion in Asia and the Pacific can work together to foster economic growth and cooperation. The strategy is implemented through a 3-year regional cooperation operations business plan (RCOBP).
