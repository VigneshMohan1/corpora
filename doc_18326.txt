Home » Hotels in India » Goa » Hotels in Calangute Hotels in Calangute Locations of Hotels in Calangute Calangute Hotels Calangute, a town situated on the northern coast of Goa, boasts of the largest beach in North Goa. The Calangute Beach, also referred to as the 'Queen of Beaches', is a major pit stop on the traveller circuit of all the beach lovers. It is the perfect place to kick back and unwind. A good place of accommodation complements the easygoing beach atmosphere and lightens the burdens for a while. The place is visited by people from the world over; however, in Calangute near the beach area, there are not ample lodging options available for tourists with a very low budget. For affordable accommodation, one can prefer a homestay in the town and enjoy the home-like atmosphere. If you are ready to spend a heavy amount, one will find multiple varieties of grand hotels and resorts.
