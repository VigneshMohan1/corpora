Rajasthan Map Location Map Physical Map Lat Long Map Outline Map District Map Road Map Railway Map Home » Hotels in India » Rajasthan » Hotels in Jaisalmer Hotels in Jaisalmer Locations of Hotels in Jaisalmer Jaisalmer Hotels A holiday in Jaisalmer, the Golden City of India lends you a chance to live and experience the real desert life amid the ethnic colours of Rajasthani culture. Tourists can find budget hotels, guest houses, heritage hotels, desert hotels, camps, etc. to spend their vacation in the city. To relish the splash of the royalty existing in the city of Jaisalmer, one must opt to stay in a heritage hotel. A wide range of heritage hotels or palaces with a fusion of historic glory and contemporary enamor is present here. However, to be a temporary resident of one of the majestic sandcastles, one has to burn a hole in his/her pocket. Some of the big-name princely homes are Fort Rajwada, Jawahar Niwas Palace and Gorbandh Palace. If you wish to witness the lightest light of the dawn as well as the darkest dark under the night sky on the golden sands of the desert, you can book a tent in the camp hotels of the city. One of the most remarkable camps set up at an incredible location, the Camp Desert Paradise extends unmatchable services to its visitors. The place is a placid haven for pure relaxation and comfort.
