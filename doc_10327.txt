Go to the shop Deccan Agro Services and Irrigation Home Deccan Agro Services and Irrigation Surat based company supplies micro irrigation systems (both drip and sprinkler irrigation systems), greenhouse house structure and greenhouse fogging system Mansukh Akbari Deccan Agro Services and Irrigation was established in 1997. The company is headquartered in Surat, Gujarat. It supplies micro irrigation systems (both drip and sprinkler irrigation systems), greenhouse house structure and greenhouse fogging system. Speaking about the specialty of their products and services, Mr. Mansukh Akbari, director, says, “We provide aftersales service after installation of irrigation systems. [hidepost] We stock all irrigation components at our godown so it is easily available to farmers whenever required. We also sell water soluble fertilizers, so that the farmers can apply it through the irrigation systems installed by us and we also provide agronomical service through our qualified agriculturists.” Mr. Akbari is an agricultural engineer. He maintains, “After visiting the field we can supply pesticides or any other required nutrients to farmers from our franchises. So we can give all services required for better farming.” He adds, “We also install greenhouse irrigation, fogging and misting systems with automation. Now we are also providing greenhouse structures to customers. We give initial information related to greenhouse /net house projects and conduct a meeting at the greenhouse site for better explanation. If a farmer or customer is interested in the project, then we provide all services like project reports, banking, installation, irrigation, temperature controlling systems inside greenhouse, and contact of plant growing company, besides others. We also provide guidance related to pests, and fertilizers.” The company markets the products by arranging customer meet, and farmer visits to farm or any agriculture related project. “We are providing better service by our well experienced staff and they help us reach target customers,” says Mr. Akbari. Greenhouse Fogging System Explaining the greenhouse fogging sytem, Mr. Akbari says, “A greenhouse is a dome which is completely closed by a GI structure covered with UV stabilized film of 200 micron polythene sheet, leading to increase in temperature. To control the temperature and increase relative humidity required by the crop fogging systems are installed. The fogger will spray 70 to 100 micron droplets of water at regular interval set through controller.” Rain Gun Agricultural Irrigation System Commenting on the rain gun irrigation system offered by the company, Mr. Akbari says, “Rain gun is available in different radii and will be used in small crop or low wind area for better results. Rain gun is required at a high pressure or horse power depending on radius or throw of water. Rain gun will be installed on a stand at the farm border after designing of farm requirement of water, and size of farm. Rain gun is required for proper filtration or flushing of pipe to reduce maintenance of nozzle.” Benefits of Drip Irrigation Speaking about the benefits of drip irrigation systems, Mr. Akbari says, “It is most beneficial in water scare areas because it saves 40 to 50% of water. Another advantage is it increases the efficiency of fertilizer. As a result there is less use of fertilizer, and minimum weed problems. You can install it on undulating land, increase soil fertility and productivity, save electricity, and save labour cost.
