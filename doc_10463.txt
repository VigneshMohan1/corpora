Go to the shop Ms. Ambika Ramesh – passion for flowers Home Ms. Ambika Ramesh – passion for flowers Passion for flowers led to career in landscape design Ms. Ambika Ramesh followed the traditional life-pattern of any Indian girl, study and get married, but she made a difference by pursuing her passion for flowers. She always kept experimenting on new ideas with flowers and started floriculture business of anthuriums and orchids in Calicut, Kerala. Presently she is involved in doing landscape designs. In her words, “I was always interested in flowers and greenery. Hence the basic lesson started from my home garden.” Later she started interacting with agricultural officers and learned more about seed collection, budding, crafting, harvesting, flower packing etc. Gradually she started participating in agri horticultural flower shows and thus increased the collection of plants. “Consecutively for 3 years I won the Star of the show award in Calicut. Inspired by these competitions, I started my business,” she adds. Climate of Calicut, Kerala (coastal region) is apt for growing orchids. Exora, Jasmine, Rose and Winka are other flowers recommended for landscaping, being rich in medicinal value and high on aesthetic features. Minimum 2000 sq ft area is required for developing a landscape. The plants can be bought from the nursery. It should be selected on the basis of climate, growth, variety, and life period. “I always check whether the plants have any fungal disease and mites, scales etc at the time of purchase,” says Ms. Ambika. Wide collection of flowering plants and effectual space utilization gave distinctive notice to Ms. Ambika in the competitive world. In addition she was awarded and honored by Kerala government on different platforms. Satisfied customers’ word of mouth publicity always brought her more work. In future she plans on creating awareness in people on growing vegetables organically in the available space in their home itself. “I shall provide all the support necessary for the same,” signs off Ms. Ambika.
