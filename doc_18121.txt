As per the 2011 Census, Tripura has a total population of 3,671,032. In the previous census of 2001 it had a population of 3,199,203. The total male population of Tripura is 1,871,867 and the total female population is 1,799,165. The population growth since the last census was 14.75 percent and the total population of the state forms 0.30 percent of the entire population in India. The area of Tripura is 10,486 sq. km with a Density of 350 per sq km. The Sex Ratio in Tripura is 961 for each 1000 male and has increased since the last census in which it was 950 per 1000 males. The child sex ratio is 953 per 1000 males and has decreased as compared to 960 in the last census. The Literacy rate in Tripura is 87.75 percent, with male and female literacy at 92.18 and 83.15 percent respectively. In figures, the total literates in Tripura are 2,831,742 out of which 1,515,973 are males and 1,315,769 are females.
