As per the 2011 Census, Gujarat has a total population of 60,383,628; previously it was 50,671,017 in 2001. Out of the total population of Gujarat , the population of male are 31,482,282 and female are 28,901,346. The total population growth in the last decade was 19.17 percent and the population of the state forms 4.99 percent of the total population in India. The total area of Gujarat is 196,024 sq. km with a Density of 308 per sq km. The Sex Ratio in Gujarat is 918 for each 1000 male. In the last census of 2001, the sex ratio was 921 per 1000 males in Gujarat. The child sex ratio is 886 per 1000 males and has decreased as compared to 964 in the 2001 census. The Literacy rate in Gujarat has risen and is 79.31 percent, with male literacy at 87.23 percent and female literacy at 70.73 percent. In figures, total literates in Gujarat are 41,948,677 out of which males are 23,995,500 and females are 17,953,177.
