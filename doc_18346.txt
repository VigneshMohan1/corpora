It is one of the picturesque hill resorts that attract nature lovers from different corners to relish the silken pleasures in the bosom of Mother Erath. In addition, the place is a hub of adventurous activities like trekking, river rafting, para gliding and skiing. Manali features hotels and lodges for tourists of different economical classes. But, if you are heading towards the hills, then make sure you book your accommodation in advance as the place is visited by huge number of tourists throughout the year, resulting in unavailability of rooms. The hotels built in the town blend harmoniously with the objects of nature, but simultaneously, they offer the best of the up-to-the-minute amenities and services to its guests. Next in the listing of criteria is the location of the hotel in the hill station. In Manali, one will find numerous hotels which offer visual treat of spectacular and heavely sunrise and sunset. Also, tourists who wish to completely escape the frenetic pace of city life, they can opt for a tented accommodation. Such an accommodation let you live in the paradise with a touch of adventure. Hotel Drilbu Manali is one of the deluxe hotels that arrange well-appointed camps amidst serenity of nature for the tourists to stay in.
