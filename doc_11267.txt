Go to the shop Tierra Food India Home Tierra Food India Producers of banana and cassava chips at Kinfra Food Park in Adoor, Kerala Tierra Food India Private Ltd is in the field of manufacturing Banana and Cassava chips and selling it as branded products. The company’s brands are Kappo for cassava chips and Banano for Banana chips. The company boasts of an automated plant except for the peeling of the raw materials. It imports most of the machines from internationally reputed manufacturers. The company invested around Rs.15 crores. Mr. Alex Thomas is the promoter and the Managing Director of the company. Mr. Kochouseph Chittilapally of V-Gurard group, Mr. C.J.George of Geojit BNP Paribas have invested in this venture. The company is located in Kinfra Food Park in Adoor, Kerala. Kinfra provides basic facilities like water, power, common ETP etc. Kinfra is an agency and is very supportive and they follow up with various Government Agencies regarding permission, licenses etc. As the company is located in Kinfra it makes easy to get permission from Government Agencies, says Mr. Thomas. The company has reformulated existing ethnic products (banana and cassava chips) making it more contemporary and palatable to youth. The company has got its own flavouring system of Kerala taste and comes out in attractive packaging. The company launched the products in Kerala three months back and is getting good response from the customers. The company’s products are available in around 15,000 outlets in Kerala. Sourcing of raw materials is the main challenge of the company and it buys from small farmers on a daily basis. Mr. Alex Thomas adds that the company is located in Kinfra Park, Adoor, in Kerala is a plus point. Since the company is situated in Kinfra Food Park, the company gets common facilities like water, power, manpower, raw materials, support from the local community etc., which is a blessing to the company. The company has got the plans to get into other ethnic snacks in the next five years. Mr. Thomas is a graduate in Chemistry. He has attended various Management Development Programmes in India and abroad to accumulate knowledge. He has got the credit of introducing first Ready-mix concrete in Cochin.
