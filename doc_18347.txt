With not many places of tourist interest, Parwanoo can be visited for a weekend to take a break from the hustle and bustle of city life. Also, it is treated as a base station for Pinjore, Kasauli, and other nearby tourist destinations. In the bosom of Mother Nature, there are several accommodations that promise a relaxing spell to the visitors. Some of the good hotels available in Parwanoo are Windsmoor, Shivalik View, Shelly and Hotel Royal Regency. In addition, one will find numerous options of backpack-type accommodation which are comfortable as well as cost-effective. Holiday spent in Parwanoo becomes worth visiting if you are able to cast out all your strains and soothe your body with the magical essence of nature. For such an experience, book a room in a hotel, such as Timber Trail Resort, that pampers you with top-drawer star services and facilities.
