Often called as the "Venice of the East" or the "Kashmiri Venice", Srinagar is blessed with the true elements of nature. Besides, numerous other assets, including the Mughal gardens, gorgeous lakes, places of worship, adventure tourism, etc. make it an irresistible tourist destination. Tourists planning a holiday in Srinagar must not worry about the availability of lodging options in the city as there are hotels to suit all the income groups, ranging from budget hotels to deluxe heritage hotels. Almost all the hotels have a travel desk to help and guide tourists in availing the best available trekking and other tour packages. A must-do thing in the city of Srinagar is to stay on a houseboat. The Kashmiri culture spread across the region is visible in the interiors as well as services provided in the houseboats. The well-furnished houseboats sailing on water serve as a haven of comfort and peace for the tourists who wish to exult in the bosom of nature. Houseboats are available at different prices depending upon their size and type. The Srinagar Houseboat Owner's Association has segregated houseboats into several categories to make your selection easy. A visit to Srinagar gives a chance to submerge oneself in a wealth of enriching experience. So, make the most of it by choosing the right place of accommodation.
