Go to the shop Sumukha Farm Seeds Home Sumukha Farm Seeds Provides seeds for poly house and net house cultivation Sumukha Farm Seeds located at Hosur, Tamil Nadu are the distributors of seeds grown in poly houses and net house cultivation. These are 1 hybrid varieties from Rijk Zwaan, The Netherlands. The yields are very good when compared with growing conditions like open field. Company started its operation in 1999. Mr. Saravana Kumar is the managing director. Product Awareness : Company counsels farmers about growing vegetables in greenhouses and Mr. Saravana Kumar feels now they are very familiar with the process, profits and yields. Training programs and classes for farmers are also arranged where technical staffs interact and guide farmers at their respective field. Sumukha Farm Seeds faces competition from Syngenta, Clause Seeds, Sakata, Enza Zaden etc. Mr. Saravana shares, “We are into contract farming. We provide all farming inputs and buy back the produce from the farmers.” He informs, “Farmers should be aware about the market conditions and planting seasons when buying seeds, which most farmers ignore. They also ignore on growing aspects, like proper fertigation and pest management.” Mr. Saravana Kumar did his graduation in English. He worked for a fertilizer company prior to starting seed business to have the practical knowledge in agriculture. Further, he travels to different countries to study new things in agriculture.
