The Chamber’s theme this year will be Development through Connectivity. Connectivity comes in many avatars: cultural and people connectivity, digital connectivity and physical connectivity through expansion of roads, railways, ports and air links. Better connectivity and people networks will lead to more innovation, creativity, business and commerce. It will also create new opportunities for all stakeholders including our members.
