Orissa Tribal Empowerment and Livelihoods Program (OTELP)   Operational Area: Koraput district in Odisha Orissa Tribal Empowerment and Livelihood Program is supported by DFID and IFAD, implemented by Government of Orissa to empower the tribals and enable them to enhance their food security to increase their incomes improve overall quality of their livelihood.   The overall approach of the program is to build the capacity of marginal groups enhance the access of poor tribal people to natural resources and increase its productivity encourage and facilitate off-farm enterprises monitor the basic entitlements of tribal households.   Chetna partnered with Government of Orissa to implement the program in Koraput- the most backward and ecologically fragile district, to implement the program benefitting 1250 families covering 3000 hectares.           Updates   Post by Chetna Organic Farmers Association,Odisha. Aug 2013: As part of the OTELP program in Boipariguda Block of Koraput district, Chetna Organic staff has been conducting PRAs and Village Devlopment and Livelihoods plan.
