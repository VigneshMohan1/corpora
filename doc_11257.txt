Go to the shop Tamil Nadu Organic Certification Department Home Tamil Nadu Organic Certification Department Offers organic certification at nominal charges for farmers in Tamil Nadu TNOCD is an organization committed to the welfare and sustainable     upliftment of the farmers of Tamil Nadu and is fully owned by the Government of Tamil Nadu. It was established in the year 2007 at Coimbatore. In case of certification charges the organization charges a fee of Rs. 1700/- for small and marginal farmers/operators, Rs. 2200/- for others and for groups the fee is Rs. 6200/-. TNOCD has more than 320 projects registered for certification as of date. Director, TNOCD explains, “there are many small farmers who are involved as family in farming activities without hiring any labor and they are very passionate, committed to the protection of nature, environment and the eco system. So organic farming should neither be considered expensive or labour intensive”. He recommends farmers to take organic certification as they can export their produce to the desired international markets. Further, the third party certification provides a guarantee and trust to the consumers which help farmers to fetch a better price for their farm produce. The consumers get poison free, nutritious and rich organic food for consumption. Due to the emergence of a number of reliable organic outlets in India, farmers get enough opportunities to sell their yield to a crowd that prefer quality over prices. He also adds, “when selecting certification agency the farmer should compare the fee structure of different certification bodies offering the certificate for a similar set of standards and also confirm about the status of accreditation and other requirements”. TNOCD is working with and part of the State Agriculture Department in Tamil Nadu. Company plans to develop system for certification of organic production under many other international standards. Director, TNOCD concludes suggesting that the mushrooming of organic product outlets should be brought under regulation.
