STEP - I In order to start selling your Product/Service, first you have to register at http://www.exportersindia.com/ by clicking the ‘ Join Free ’ link (displayed at the top of the Home Page ExportersIndia.Com ) and become a member To become a member, just fill the provided form and click on ‘ Join Now Free ’ and then you will be taken to your ‘My Folder’ In ‘My Folder’, first go to the ‘Manage Profile’ tab and click on ‘Edit Company Information’ as well as “Edit Contact Details” for updating your profile After updating your profile, go to ‘My Listings’ and then click ‘Add New Listing’ from the dropdown menu Now, just select an appropriate business category e.g. Manufacturer, Exporter, Supplier, Service Provider etc. as per your choice and fill the relevant text into Box and then click on "Submit" button. STEP - II Search OR Browse related ‘ Buy Offers ’ and ‘ Buyers ’ at ExportersIndia.Com and contact them through " Inquiry Form " Selling through – "Buy Offers" At ExportersIndia.com home page, just click on any desired category in the " Browse by Category " section Now, click on the " Buy Leads " tab in the header section And finally, choose the most specific Buy Offer(s) from display to get enquiries from the listed company " Recent Leads Offers to Buy " Selling through – "Trade Leads" At ExportersIndia.com , a user can find a number of Trade Leads which cater to diverse industries. For this, Just click on " Trade Leads " link at the home page From the result page, you can browse the latest Buy/Sell Offers (Indian Buy Offers as well as International Buy Offers etc.) Additionally, you can also click on an industry-specific category in " Trade Leads By Category " section Selling through – "Importers / Buyers search" As per you choice, either click on " Indian Classified " tab or " Foreign Classifieds " tab Then, just enter any desired keyword in search bar and select "Importers" from the search bar dropdown Now browse a category from the alphabetical display Advertising & Premium Membership Why Advertise with ExportersIndia.com ?
