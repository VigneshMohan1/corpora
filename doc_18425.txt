Apart from being the biggest industrial and commercial centre in South India, the alluring city of Chennai houses several marvels built in south Indian architecture, charming beaches, prominent spiritual edifices, centres for fun & entertainment, etc., thereby, businessmen as well as tourists from India and abroad flock to the city. From a wide array of hotels in Chennai, the list of cost-effective accommodation includes Ampa Crystal Inn, L R Swami Hotels Pvt Ltd, Krishna Residency, Picnic Hotel Pvt Ltd, Sudha Inn Pvt Ltd., while Madras Hotel Ashoka, Hotel Victoria, Southern Crest, Sangeetha Residency are the hotels that provide star services. For more details about the cost and services of different hotels, one must refer to the list given below. The ITC Grand Chola Hotel, a 5-star deluxe hotel, is the largest hotel in South India and the largest LEED-certified green hotel in the world. The splendid facilities with outstanding hospitality assure a perfect stay to their guests. The incredible location of the hotel, i.e., in the heart of the city adds to the conveniences of the tourists as well as business travellers. It is advisable to pre-book a room for yourself, if you are visiting the city during the peak season. For tourists, the best time to visit Chennai is in the months of November to February as during the summer months, it is difficult to bear the heat in Chennai and move around to explore the tourist destinations. Also, during the peak season, many cultural fests are organised in the city drawing in greater number of tourists to the city. So, if you plan a holiday during these months, you must book your accommodation in advance.
