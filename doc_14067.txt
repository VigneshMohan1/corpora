Posted in About Finer Twitter We would like to introduce ourselves as the Federation of Industry & Commerce of North Eastern Region (FINER), formed for the creation and sustenance of an environment conducive to the growth of Industry, Trade and Commerce in the North Eastern States of India. FINER has also established fruitful connection with Chambers of Commerce across various regions and promoted its relationship with Governments; Semi-Governments; Industrial and Commercial organizations and enterprises both at home and abroad. Needles to mention, FINER is the most vibrant Apex Industrial body of the N.E. Region. FINER has found representation in practically all-advisory committees of the State and Central Governments pertaining to industry, trade and commerce of the region. FINER’s views are frequently sought by the Government and media. FINER’s sole aim is to industrially develop the region and bring about an economic resurgence. The twenty-one years since inception has put FINER in a position of credibility where its voice is heard and heeded. FINER Chairman, Shri R.S. Joshi , is also a Member of the Special Task Force of the Prime Minister on MSME at New Delhi.  FINER has also signed an MOU with The North East Members of Parliament Forum and meetings have been regularly held with them in Delhi.    FINER has established global connections with Chambers of Commerce and Industry across the nations of the world. Most recently the MOU with the Brazil Chambers of Commerce, and Chambers of Commerce at Turkey, to add to the already established relations with Bangladesh, Bhutan, Singapore, China to name a few.
