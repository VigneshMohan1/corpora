outcomes achieved in its operations in 2014, with most completed on budget, says an ADB performance review released today. Development Effectiveness Review 2014 Report The 2014 Development Effectiveness Review tracks recent development progress in Asia and the Pacific, assesses ADB’s development effectiveness, and identifies areas where ADB’s performance needs to be strengthened. Ecotourism and Agriculture Help Save Wetlands Habitat in the People's Republic of China In the Sanjiang Plain, People's Republic of China, ADB has helped to restore thousands of hectares of wetlands while protecting and improving the livelihoods of farmers. Clean Water Improves Health, Breaks Down Barriers in Nepal A community-based project supported by ADB has brought clean water and better sanitation to more than 568,000 people in Nepal. Tourism Boom Fuels Business Opportunities in Mekong Subregion ADB has helped turn former war-torn Greater Mekong Subregion countries of Cambodia, Lao PDR, and Viet Nam into a booming tourist destination. Better Banking Practices Boost Business, Create Jobs in Azerbaijan’s Diversifying Economy ADB’s partnership with an Azerbaijan bank has promoted better banking practices and helped entrepreneurs flourish in a country that has rocketed to upper middle-income status. Facebook Twitter LinkedIn Print Bookmark Overview Providing the right development aid is not only about the size of the loan or the number of roads or schools built. It is about knowing the loan is improving people’s lives in a sustainable way. It is about ensuring that the school educates children with skills to support a country’s economic future, or that when a new highway is built, it not only spurs growth and fosters trade but also brings tangible benefits to surrounding communities.
