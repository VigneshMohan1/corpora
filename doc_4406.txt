Our students not only obtain a solid education in the classroom, but are also among the most dedicated and enthusiastic student leaders on the OSU campus. They are self-starters who demonstrate strong leadership, communication, and team building skills through the various student activities and organizations in the College. In addition, employers continually compliment our students on their strong work ethic and character.
