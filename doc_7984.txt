ELASIA Trade Show India ELASIA Start Date   29-May-2015   End Date   01-Jun-2015 Nesco Compound, Off Western Express Highway, Goregaon East, Maharashtra Mumbai, India Tel : +91-22-66450123 ELASIA Electronics & Electrical 29 May - 01 Jun 2015 Event Profile ELASIA is the biggest International Exhibition focused on power, electrical & industrial electronics. It will provide an unparalleled global forum for product information, first hand data and also to create business links. It will bring together the top leaders of the industry on a common platform. Exhibitor Profile The targeted exhibitors at Indiamart ELASIA are companies/firms dealing in: equipment for generation, transmission, distribution, protection and utilization of electrical power such as generators, transformers, alternators, motors & motor winding equipment, switchgears and control gear equipment such as circuit breakers, isolators and relays, capacitors, instrument transformers, transmission line towers, cables, insulators, winding wires etc. Visitor Profile The targeted visitors at Indiamart ELASIA 2009 are professionals related to the following industries: power, electronic devices, instrumentation & control, microwave components & subsystems, packaging, test & measuring instruments, medical systems, telecommunications etc. Book Your Stall General Inquiry Get Sponsorship Visitor Registration Organizer Info Contact Organizer Triune Exhibitors Private Limited Address :- 25, 3rd Floor, 8th Main Road, Vasanthnagar, , Bangalore , Karnataka [ India ] Phone : +91-80-22352770 Fax : +91-80-22352772 Trade Shows by Month May 2015 Jun 2015 Jul 2015 Aug 2015 Sep 2015 Oct 2015 Nov 2015 Dec 2015 Jan 2016 Feb 2016 Mar 2016 Apr 2016 Trade Shows by Country Argentina Australia China Germany France India Italy Japan South Korea Mexico Russian Federation United Kingdom United States WELCOME GUEST Join Us!
