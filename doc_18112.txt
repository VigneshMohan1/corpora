According to the 2011 Census, the total population of Meghalaya is 2,964,007. Previously, the total population was 2,318,822 in the 2001 Census. The male population is 1,492,668 and the female population is 1,471,339 from the entire population of Meghalaya. The total population growth since the last decade was 27.82 percent and the population of the state forms 0.24 percent of the entire population in India. Meghalaya has an area of 22,429 sq. km with a Density of 132 per sq km. The Sex Ratio in Meghalaya is 986 for each 1000 male and has increased from 975 per 1000 males since the last census. The child sex ratio is 970 per 1000 males and has increased as compared to 932 in the last census. Meghalaya has a Literacy rate of 75.48 percent, with male literacy at 77.17 percent and female literacy at 73.78 percent. In figures, the total literates in Meghalaya are 1,817,761 out of which the males are 934,091 and females are 883,670 respectively.
