One of the most sought after hill stations in northern India, Kasauli helps you rejuvenate your senses amidst the soothing elements of nature-the fuel for the soul. Visiting Kasauli with friends or family, one must prefer a hotel in a serene and a peaceful location to help you escape the din and bustle of city life. Kasauli has well equipped resorts like Kasauli Regency Hotel which organise Silent Disco or Headphone Party for its guests. Hotels in Kasauli are also famous for rooftop bars and restaurants. Other facilities provided at such deluxe hotels include gym area, spa, games area, laundry service, parking area, etc. There are number of options for the travellers with a shoestring budget as well in the green valley. One can get a basic but a clean accommodation at a reasonable tariff. In addition, the hotel staff helps you with the booking of trekking activities.
