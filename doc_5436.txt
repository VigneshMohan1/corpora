The Functions of Division are: Policy formulation and administration of the following Acts of Parliament:- Insurance Act, 1938 Life Insurance Corporation Act, 1956 General Insurance Business (Nationalization) Act, 1972 IRDA Act, 1999 Actuaries Act, 2006 Constant review and monitoring of the performance of the National Insurance Companies. Framing of rules and regulations in respect of service conditions of employees of Public sector Insurance Companies. Appointment of Chief Executives and Directors on the Board of Public sector Insurance Companies and on the IRDA. Implementation of government sponsored insurance scheme like Universal Health insurance scheme, Aam Aadmi Bima Yojana, Varisht Pension Yojana Etc.
