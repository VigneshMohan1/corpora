Sonitpur ( Assam ) OR Application Format can be downloaded from the website OR Application on plain paper with the necessary information like Name, Address, Date of Birth, Category, Educational Qualification, Technical Qualification along with all Testimonials may be sent well in advance before commencement of the course. For the Sponsored Nominees: The sponsoring organization may select the candidates to be nominated for the different courses alongwith brief bio-data of each candidate and send it to the institute well in advance. For Academic Level Courses: The educational Institutes may nominate their students undergoing Diploma/ Degree courses in Agricultural Engineering and send it to the Institute before February in each year to enable us to organize their training in the month of May, June and July. The brief bio-data of each nominee must have their name and ID number, home address, etc.
