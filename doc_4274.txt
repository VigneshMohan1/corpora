Jain Institute of Management SP Jain Institute of Management Overall Rating:  [ 3 / 5 ]Total Votes [  7  ]    Rate this page: 1 2 3 4 5 The SP Jain Institute of Management & Research (SPJIMR) owned by Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan is regarded as being among the leading 10 b-schools of India. Margaret Thatcher, the erstwhile British Prime Minister had founded the institute in 1981. It has received the following accreditations since its establishment: Entity Accreditation received All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) for the PGDM program The Association of MBAs (AMBA), London, UK PGPM PGEMP PGDM National Board of Accreditation (NBA) PGDM program given an A Grade Crisil Rating Rated A***B-school for both state and national levels Association of Indian Universities (AIU) PGDM program certified to be at par with traditional MBA programs offered by Indian universities Its major achievements may be mentioned as below: Chosen by the Wadhwani Foundation to be a National Entrepreneurship Network (NEN) with IIM(A), BITS (Pilani), IIT (Mumbai) and IBAB (Bangalore) Has partnered IBM for Services Science and Management related research The sole institute in Asia that has received an invitation from the US based Aspen Institute for integration of CSR in management studies and developing the structure for Teaching Improvement Program (TIP). It was one of the 12 institutions from around the world that were selected for the purpose.
