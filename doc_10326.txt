Your shopping bag is empty. Go to the shop Dayal Fertilizers Group – fertilizers Home Dayal Fertilizers Group – fertilizers Meerut based producers of chemical and organic fertilizers. Also manufacturing animal feed and seeds Dr. Jitendra Kumar Singh Dayal Fertilizers Group established in 1979 began its operation with manufacturing Zinc Sulfate and now has diversified to organic and bio fertilizers, animal feed, poultry feed, and seeds with complete scientific solutions for different types of crop cultivation according to the agro-climatic zone. All products are sold under the brand name “Dayal”. Located at Meerut, Uttar Pradesh. The company has been certified by ECOCERT under NPOP for the eco-friendly products. Dr. Jitendra Kumar Singh is the senior manager of the company. Company is working on Integrated Plant Nutrients Management and Integrated Pest Management which is the need of the hour for best eco-friendly farming. According to Dr. Singh, Dayal Group has adequate infrastructure available for its R & D activities along with strong research team with wide experience. The animal feed division at Dayal Group is vast, having numerous products sold in dairy markets in urban and semi-urban areas. Challenges Company holds strong base amongst Indian farmers and has to put in umpteen efforts to boost its organic and bio-products in market by training the field staff thus having better reach amongst the target customers. However, when it comes to exports, China is the biggest challenge and company has to work hard to maintain the quality to stay in the competition. Future Plans It has entered into exports with African countries and plans in a big way in the coming years. The main focus shall be on seeds hence with assistance of company’s retired scientists it is working on establishing strong R & D facility in seeds division. Presently, the major brand name is ‘Dayal’ but it is being planned to separate names for each crops and varieties, which is in progress. It also plans to market semi-chemical pesticides and herbal pesticides apart from tissue culture products. Dr. Singh is a post graduate with Ph.D in Agricultural Sciences with more than 12 years of experience in agribusiness companies.
