FAO   collaborates and works with national governments as lead national focal institutions and with the proactive participation of farming communities and other local organizations, such as youth organizations, women’s organizations, research centres and academes, producer groups and cooperatives, and other relevant organizations. GIAHS networks and strategic partnerships between public and private sector shall be further established to promote inter-institutional, inter-region and inter-country collaboration to enhance and promote revitalization of agricultural heritage systems and its dynamic conservation.
