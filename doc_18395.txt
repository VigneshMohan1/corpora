Mahabaleshwar is located in the Satara district in Maharashtra. Being a popular holiday resort, a honeymoon spot as well as a holy place for Hindus, the place is visited by tourists throughout the year. In a hill-station, the location of the hotel is one of the big assets which must be considered while you choose your accommodation. Hotels poised against the lush green mountain ranges offer you a peaceful and serene view to glance at. Ranging from hotels of economical rates to 3-star hotels, one can find hotels within a reachable distance of tourist destinations in Mahabaleshwar. Depending upon the amount you spent, you can avail the facilities at your choice of hotel.
