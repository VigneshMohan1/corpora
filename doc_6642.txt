Sign up here to receive email updates about the below ways to get involved :   Volunteer with NAF Academies NAF will help you connect to advisory boards and other volunteer opportunities in your area, like attending career days and speaking to current NAF students about your experiences.   Take Part in Networking Events NAF provides you with opportunities to network with other alumni in your region and industry. Alumni Week is an opportunity for alumni across the country to network, celebrate each other's accomplishments, and encourage others to get involved! Click here to learn more. Win an Alumni Honoree Award The Alumni Awards recognize NAF alumni who have achieved success in college or their careers, thanks in part to their academy experience. This year’s awards focus on how industry collaboration has helped alumni navigate their career paths, and how alumni are a source of encouragement for NAF academies and their communities. Apply for the NAF Alumni Business Scholarship Alumni and/or graduating seniors pursuing further education in business and/or financial services and who are involved with their local academy will receive an award of $1,500 to be used to further their studies. For more information on this and other scholarships, click here . Attend the Annual NAF Benefit Each year, the National Academy Foundation’s Benefit attracts top business executives from leading global companies who come together with educators and students to celebrate lifelong success for young people.
