Based at Mumbai, Maharashtra Shyam Bhadane Plant Genome Sciences (PGS) is a leading marketer of plant nutrients, hydroponic equipment, soil conditioning agents and greenhouse structures. PGS is into hydroponics business, besides undertaking turnkey projects it also provides agronomic knowhow. The company is located in Andheri (East), Mumbai in Maharashtra state. Plant Genome Sciences Pvt. Ltd, Mumbai, India is an authorized distributor of Advanced Nutrients Canada. Mr. Shyam Bhadane is the Manager – Agriculture Department. Hydroponics is new to India. Farmers are not ready to accept this technology easily. It requires a lot of explanation to convince the farmers about the new technology. PGS shows to farmers greenhouses with hydroponics cultivation and also briefs them how the nutrients are applied and how it works at different stages. The company does not manufacture the product here, but imports from Canada. PGS believes that Research & Development and application of knowledge will enhance and sustain agricultural industries and protect environment. PGS are in the field of hydroponics, the products are developed through extensive and rigorous testing by a team of world-class research scientists. The products are specifically engineered and formulated to work based on plants’ genetic make-up, and PGS is one of the few companies that completely understands and comprehends the need for high-yielding and fast-growing plants. PGS is set up with a vision to provide quality plant nutrients, innovative technological know-how and all-inclusive end-to-end solutions to the evolving agriculture industry in Asia, Africa and the Middle East. Mr. Shyam Bhadane is a B. Tech in Agricultural Engineering. He has worked in the field of irrigation, greenhouses, automation, vertical farming / green wall etc. Mr. Shyam says “our company’s aim is to encourage farmers to adopt Integrated Pest Management (IPM), new crop varieties, Good Agricultural Practices (GAP) and adoption of latest technology. We are committed to bring a revolution in the field of agriculture in Asia & Africa through innovative and scientific techniques like hydroponics and technologically advanced hydroponic equipments and supplies.’’ “We are planning to have a manufacturing unit here in the next few years. Right now our Canada office is supplying to all over the World. We want farmers to understand hydroponics better. We are planning to conduct demonstrations at some strategic locations and will train the farmers who are interested. I am very optimistic about hydroponics/ aeroponics technology and sure this will provide solutions for lots of problems,’’ adds Mr. Shyam.
