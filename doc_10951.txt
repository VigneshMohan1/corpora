Go to the shop Sun Infratech – contract farming Home Sun Infratech – contract farming New Delhi based firm specialises in contract farming, plantation supervision, protected and open field cultivation of exotic plants Sun Infratech Pvt. Ltd is an agro based company specializing in contract farming, plantation supervision, protected and open     field cultivation of exotic plants, nursery/plantations, and dairy farming. Sun also provides consultancy services in the above agricultural practices and gets certifications done through its consultancy wing. The company is headquartered in New Delhi. Mr. Nilesh Singh is the managing director of the company. Mr. Singh is a commerce graduate. “At present the company is doing contract farming on 4000 acres of land across India, and in future we plan to expand overseas,” he adds. Certification For the products that the company sells in the market, Sun gets the certification done from SGS Organic. “Organic retail of products, processing units, and agricultural land are the products of agriculture we work on for the certification,” says Mr. Singh. “We mainly focus on agricultural products. The other sectors that we focus on are trading activities, dairy products, and processed foods,” he adds. Further speaking about the advantages of certification, he says, “Certification adds value to the product, increases the brand value and brand recognition in the market, which results in better market price for the products.” Agro Consultancy The company’s agro consultancy includes the following services: · Farm visit · Collection of soil sample · Analyses of environmental conditions · In-house project development · Farm Preparation ·Crop selection · Advise on use of pesticides and fertilizers · Advise on use of protective cultivation · Integrated pest and nutrient management · Farm certification · Hi-tech nursery · Warehouse to protect post-harvest and finally · Increase income to farming community Current Agricultural Projects The current agricultural projects handled by the company are: 1. Organic Farming: The organic farming site is located in Greater Noida. Various types of vegetables, fruits, cereals and pulses are being cultivated there. 2. Contract Farming: Sun has an agreement with Sriram DCM group at Pali, Rajasthan, for cultivation of mustard seeds. Aloe Vera is also being cultivated in this site. 3. Dairy Farming: Sun is setting up a dairy farm having more than 225 cows. 4. Jatropha Cultivation: The Company is into plantation of jatropha on 2200 acres land in three districts of Chhattisgarh. It is a joint venture with Indian Oil Corporation Ltd., and Chhattisgarh Renewable Energy Development Agency (CREDA). Certification Process in India Speaking about the certification process in India, Mr. Singh says, “It is very slow if we compare with the developed countries. There are so many factors attributed to this, including small land holdings and lack of awareness, besides others.” He suggests, “The government should promote contract farming, co-operative movements, initial capital investments for getting the farms certifications, educating the people on root level to part of this movement with so many other measures,” adding, “The government has taken some steps including major decision of allowing producer companies formation and also there are different plans from different government bodies for capital subsidies for farmers. But these measures are inadequate and require more attention.” Our Correspondent For more information, contact : Mr. Nilesh Singh, Managing Director Sun Infratech Pvt.
