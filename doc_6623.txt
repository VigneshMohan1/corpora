A small gift each month can have a tremendous impact.   Special Giving Opportunities To discuss special giving opportunities and find out how you can make a lasting investment on the education of NAF academy students, please contact our institutional advancement team .   Matching Gift and Employee Giving Campaigns Add NAF to your company's employee giving campaign. You can elect to give through ongoing payroll deductions or pledge a one-time gift to NAF. Plus, many companies will match your contribution, helping your support go even further. Be sure to talk to your company’s HR department for more details.   Tribute Gifts Making a gift in honor of a loved one is a unique way to show you care.  By selecting one of tribute giving options from the online donation page , you can make a tax-deductible donation in the name of a family member or friend and we will send a letter letting them know about your generous gift.   Donations of Stock Donate stock shares and make a secure, expedient, and cost effective contribution towards education. For more information on donating stock, please contact our institutional advancement team .
