Go to the shop Sprout Natural India – Mushroom cultivation Home Sprout Natural India – Mushroom cultivation Practices concept of Farm to Plate, supplies fruits and vegetables to restaurants and hotels Sprout Natural India, a Pune based company, was established in March 2011. Mr. Anil Wanarse Patil is the founder.     The company is engaged in production and marketing of strawberry, sweet corn, and baby corn; marketing of seasonal fruits like grapes, pomegranate, and mango; and marketing of exotic fruits and vegetables. It is also actively involved in institutional marketing of fruits and vegetables; production and marketing of cereals, pulses and oil seeds. Consultancy services for mushroom and banana cultivation and market research of agri projects are the other activities undertaken by Sprout Natural India. The company’s verticals in a nutshell can be classified into: • Agribusiness • Food and Agribusiness Consulting, • Sprout Market Research • Recruitment & HR Services The company has separate wings to produce and supply organic food. This business takes around 30 % of the share of its total sales and business. The remaining 70% constitutes using inorganic fertilizers at actual. Sprout India targets restaurants and hotels as customers. It secures their market position and also provides high return for their investment. “Our model is based on the concept of Farm to Plate enabling managing comprehensive supply chain for produce,” Mr. Patil shares. The farms of the company are managed as per the specifications of produce mentioned by these customers. The current model undertaken by the company has a huge scope in the Indian market. It enables the production of quality, fresh, and middle-men free F & V and supply to the market. Mr. Patil goes on to share the challenges faced in organic farming in general. “Indian farms use excess fertilizers and they need to drain out from the soil. Further, in a country like India, we face food scarcity, and hence organic food is not an option. Only certain classes with high disposable income prefer organic food.” Sprout Natural also holds a joint venture with Natures Agrotech. The company is one of the leading companies in supply of fresh, processed and organic food to overseas market. Upon seeing Sprout Natural’s business model, quality produce and timely service, the company has received export orders and further association between the two has continued. One of the major aims behind the company’s formation was to establish itself extensively in the Indian market with client satisfaction through quality agriculture produce production, marketing and food and agribusiness consulting services. Within a short span of two years it has made its mark in the agriculture domain. Mr. Patil lists one such achievement: “We received the Best Agri Project Consultation Award – FY 2013-14 from Dr. Amit Saini, District Collector, for setup and consulting one of the biggest mushroom farms, medicinal plantation commercial cultivation and other agribusiness feasibility consulting process.” By linking India’s food and agribusiness to the world, Sprout Naturals hopes to help farmers, corporate, individuals and cooperatives to get their food and agribusiness simplified. Mr. Patil holds a B.Sc. (Agriculture), PGDABPM in agribusiness and plantation management from Indian Institute of Plantation Management, Bangalore. He worked as an Asst. Manager (operations) in Interavion India Pvt Ltd, Bangalore, for a year before setting up Sprout Naturals.
