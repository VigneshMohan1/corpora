It has won appreciation from the world over for it seems that nature has adorned Shimla with graceful charms for the purpose of rejuvenating our senses. Some of the attractive tourist destinations in Shimla include Jakhu Hill, Christ Church, Jakhu Temple, Chadwick Falls, Shimla Water Catchment Sanctuary, Hip Hip Hurray Amusement Park, and Dorje Drag Monastery. Shimla has always been one of the top honeymoon destinations in India. Besides, the place is frequently visited for recreation as well as business trips. The destination remains a tourist delight throughout the year, thus the lodges have been designed to suit the requirements of the visitors in all seasons. During the off-season, the hotels put forward good discounts, ranging from 30-50% cut on tariff, but in the peak season, the rooms are available at soaring rates. However, a lodging option perched on the top of a hill is the first choice of tourists as it allows a bird's eye view of the town. Nature lovers can relish the visual treat of spectacular and heavenly sunrise and sunset. One such hotel with incredible location is the Wildflower Hall. The hotel with superlative amenities and services flaunts grace and eminence against the backdrop of the mighty mountain chain. A paradise with a touch of adventure, Shimla is also visited by huge number of people because the place offers several adrenaline-pumping activities for the travellers. To guide and assist tourists in booking the best available packages for trekking, river rafting, Skiing, etc. most of the hotels have a travel desk. Also, the hotels in Shimla facilitate advance booking for the convenience of the tourists, rather some hotels have made pre-booking mandatory as it prevents any kind of ruckus.
