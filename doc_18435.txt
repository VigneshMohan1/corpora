From lodging to shopping, the town provides a wide range of options for the tourists. Tourists travelling to Nainital will find comfortable accommodations at prices that will be fairly easy on wallet. However, for a luxurious stay in hotels with distinct services and facilities, a traveller will have to pay a greater amount. Also, one can spend his holiday in a state-run tourist guest-house by spending a reasonable amount. The main attraction of the hill station is its Naini Lake. Thereby, to make the most of your stay in Nainital, travellers should prefer to book a lakeside hotel that looks out over the beautiful stretch of water. During the peak season, i.e., from the month of May to July, it is advisable to pre-book your accommodation to avoid any kind of mess. In case you happen to visit the town during off-season, then you must avail hefty discounts and offers at the hotels.
