Chetna and a Farmer Cooperative – Kamadhenu Society in Yavatmal promoted by Chetna has been provisionally selected as project implementation agencies to cover 13,200 poor and poorest households in three clusters with financial outlay of over INR.150 million in next 4 four years.    As on date, Chetna and /or its partner Kamdhenu have not applied/opted for SRTT funds for technical/political reasons.
