ADB’s Open Access Website Now Online ADB has made all its economic and development research on Asia and the Pacific available under open access, a principle that promotes unrestricted online access to scholarly research so that it can be more widely distributed and used. The ADB Open Access Repository (OAR) will eventually include the complete current and back catalog of ADB research—well over 5,000 publications—dating back to 1966, the year ADB was established.
