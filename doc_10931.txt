Go to the shop Space Consultancy Services Home Space Consultancy Services Jabalpur based consultants offering services for establishing new business operations Space Consultancy Services (SCS) was set up in 2008 to offer integrated technical consultancy services in India and International market. Mr. Arvind Tiwari is founder of the company. Located at Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh. Services SCS offers services for business start-up right from providing basic information to technology evaluation, sourcing and assimilation of detailed project reports, market survey studies and research. It aims to provide business solutions to its clients to reduce risk and give high return on application investments. Company also offers wide range of project information CDs in all agri-related segments, such as greenhouse farming, hydroponic culture, pearl culture, aloe vera cultivation, stevia, safed musli, goat farming, dairy farming, poultry farming etc. Mr. Virendra Singroure, Manager at Space Consultancy Services, says, “there are so many starters who want to start their projects but due to lack of knowledge they are unable to start. These CDs are compilation of collected project information through consultants and engineers with the latest available data for specific project, helpful for beginners.” CDs assists the user to analyze the market potential of the project. Challenges Changing technology has been the biggest challenge for SCS. Mr. Virendra Singroure is M.Tech with more than 4 years of experience in the field of Market Research.
