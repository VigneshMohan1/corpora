Objectives The main objectives of the scheme are: a) to improve the database of inland and marine fisheries resources and catch of fish using the statistical sound and scientific methodology b) to improve Geographical Information System in the States/UTs other concerned implementing agencies c) registration of all fishing vessels and d) to carry out census on marine fisheries Salient features The scheme would improve the efficiency and quality of data as well as reduction in time lag in preparation and submission of survey vessels/reports, fish production of both inland and marine in all districts in the States and UTs could be estimated, Provide comprehensive data base of fishery sector so as to facilitate planning process at the State and Central level   Structure of the scheme The scheme is being operated with the following components:   (i) Sample Survey for Estimation of Inland Fishery Resources and their Potential and Fish Production (ii) Census of Marine Fisheries (iii) Catch Assessment Surveys for Inland and Marine Fisheries (iv) Development of Geographical Information System of the Fisheries Sector (v) Assessment of Fish Production Potential in Coastal Areas (vi) Evaluation Studies/Professional Services (vii)Registration of Fishing Vessels (viii)Development of Database of Fisheries Cooperative of India (ix) Mapping of Smaller Water Bodies and Development of GIS based Fishery Management System (x)Strengthening of Statistical Unit at Headquarters Funding pattern The Central assistance is 100% grant-in-aid to State Government/UT Administrations and other implementing agencies. Eligibility State Govt and UT Admn. Target Group/Beneficiaries State Govt and UT Admn. Area of operation Inland and marine fishery resources of all the States and UTs Implementing agency State Governments /UT Administrations, CMFRI, FSI, CIFRI and FISCOPFED. Procedure for applying Implementing agencies are required to submit the proposal to DAHDF.
