It will be hosted by Bangladesh and will be played in three cities - Dhaka, Sylhet, and Chittagong. 16 teams have qualified for this tournament. 8 teams have directly qualified for Super 10. Two top most teams out of the 8 qualified teams of group stage will join the Super 10.
