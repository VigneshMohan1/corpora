Go to the shop Agri Unlimited Home Agri Unlimited Bengaluru, Karnataka based traders of floriculture and horticulture related products Kalidas Raj Agri Unlimited was formed in 2009 to provide marketing solutions to companies and individual farmers dealing in different field of agriculture. Located at Bengaluru, Karnataka, it specializes in Floriculture, Horticulture, Organic farming, Sustainable agriculture, Joint ventures, Turnkey consultancies and co-operative farming. Ongoing projects are at Kodagu district, Bangalore, Tamil Nadu and Srinagar. Mr. Kalidas Raj is Proprietor of the company. He holds degree in Agriculture from University of Agriculture Science, GKVK, Bangalore. He explains, “ we procure different agri products mainly the flowers, fruits and flowers from farmers, clusters and cooperatives. We are the distributors for planting materials of Gerbera, Carnation, Orchids, Cut foliage etc for South India and represent companies like Rise and Shine Flori Tech, Pune, Spic Ltd Coimbatore.” Areas of Operation : Bengaluru and its neighboring cities are the main area of operations for Agri Unlimited and it also exports to Middle East countries. Mr. Kalidas Raj shares, “we regularly monitor the demand and supply of our products. Later based on the requirement we suggest the farmers to take up crops having good demand.” Storage and Packaging : Company deals mainly in perishable items, having short life hence maintains a complete cold chain system for vegetables. “ We have packing and grading hall, cold room and refer van to transport the vegetables. For flowers we have cold storage to pre cool and storage,” says Mr. Kalidas Raj. Milestones : In floriculture, Agri Unlimited has introduced new varieties of fresh cut flowers and cut foliage’s like Chrysanthemum, Hydrangea, Peony, Orchids, Anthuriums, Leather leaf fern, Tropical flowers and Foliage’s. In horticulture the company has introduced new variety of pomegranate – California wonder from Israel, made clusters and co-operatives for growing organic fruits and vegetables at Kodagu district and has helped the farming community to take up processing, packing and marketing. For other verticals of agriculture it has helped farmers to standardize the growing practice for various Cereals, Pluses and other commercial crops in natural / organic way without using inorganic chemicals and provided assistance to them in marketing these products. Challenges : Company faces major unsolvable issues mainly non-availability of quality water, shortage of power supply and non-availability of manpower due to urbanization and increase in the cost of inputs. Olive Farming : “Presently in India, Olive farming is in the stage of mass planting after various trials for past few years. There is lot of demand for the product and once the yield starts, various measures will be taken for collection, processing and marketing. For olive farming, location selected must be suitable for flowering,” concludes Mr. Kalidas Raj.
