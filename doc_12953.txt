My Folder Home Help My Account & Sign In My Folder "My Folder" is a unique profile folder that you get after registering yourself with ExportersIndia.com. Through ‘My Folder’, you can manage your entire portfolio on the portal. This folder brings you different options and features to manage your account as per your requirement. The different features, available in your My Folder, include: - My Profile   My Inquiries In this section, you can manage your business information, Login and contact details Edit Contact Information Edit Company Information Change E-Mail ID Change Password Remove My Account   In this section, you can manage your business inquires: - Classified Inquiry Trade Leads Inquiry Inquiry Delete By User My Listings   My Products In this section, you can manage your business listings as per your business type i.e. Manufacturers / Exporters / Suppliers / Importers / Service Providers etc. Here, you can: - Add New Listing Manage Listings Edit/Delete Listing   In this section, you can Add/Delete/Manage your Products, create product groups: - Add New Product Manage Products Manage Product's Groups Manage Group's Products Deleted Products My Services   My Offers In this section, you can Add/Delete/Manage your services Add New Services Manage Services   In this section, you can Add/Delete/Manage your Trade Offers (Buy Offers / Sell Offers) Add New Trade Offer Manage Trade Offers Activated Trade Offers Deleted Trade Offers My Subscription   My Tools In this section, you can manage and check your Paid Subscriptions for: - Banner Advertising My Advertisement Trade Lead Premium Display Email Marketing SMS Marketing   Apart from all this, you also get certain kinds of exclusive tools at ExportersIndia.com My Feedback Google Maps E-mail/ SMS Subscribe Alert Recruitment Solution Advertising & Premium Membership Why Advertise with ExportersIndia.com ?
