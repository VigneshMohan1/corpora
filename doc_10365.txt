Plans to offer more horticulture related products in coming years Sandeep Vidwans GBC India, based in Gujarat, offers solutions especially for hydroponics. It also imports leca. Mr. Sandeep Vidwans is the CEO of the company. The annual turnover of the company is Rs. 10 lakhs. The company operates in North and South India. In a highly competitive market, features such as high porosity, low bulk density, thermal conditioning at the root level, high cation-anion exchange, chemically inert, high silica, decreasing pathogens and flushing salt from soil set its products apart from the rest. People related to the horticulture industry are the company’s target customers. These customers approach the company through its website. The company relies heavily on its website to create awareness of its products and to attract new customers. Distribution network is a major challenge that the company faces when it comes to sale of its products. “Our products are affordable in long terms, but high investment initially is needed,” Mr. Vidwans admits. GBC India hopes to add more products and services in horticulture in the future. Mr. Sandeep Vidwans is a science graduate. He holds two to three years of experience in the horticulture industry.
