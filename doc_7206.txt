Academies that operate with fidelity to the Academy Standards do a better job of preparing their students for the next step. To attain a high level of implementation, NAF academies participate in a Continuous Improvement Cycle. The Data Center and Academy Assessment enable academies to assess academy programs and student outcomes, analyze results, plan based on their analysis, and implement action plans.
