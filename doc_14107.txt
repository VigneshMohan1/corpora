Posted in Gallery Twitter Interactive meet with  Thai   Delegation Federation of Industry and Commerce of North Eastern Region (FINER) had organized an interactive meet with a high level  Thai   delegation   headed by Shri P. Pibulsonggram, Retd Ambassador of  Thailand  & Representative of ASEAN Connectivity Coordinating Committee of  Thailand  from the Ministry of Foreign affair, Government of  Thailand  alongwith with Captains from Trade & Commerce of the North East Region today at the FINER Secretariat. The main objective of the meeting was to discuss with Captains of Industries from different sectors about the  opportunities and potential of trade & commerce in the North East India and  also to promote and enhance future business relationships between both the Countries and share the vision and policies of the governments of the of India and  Thailand  respectively.  Potential areas where investment can be made in the North East Region like Tourism, Food Processing, Organic  farming , Water Manager , Education, Waterways and Fashion Industry were discussed. The issues related to Trade and Commerce in both the Countries was also discussed.
