Go to the shop Shrushti Hitech and Agriculture Development Home Shrushti Hitech and Agriculture Development Provides green house for floriculture projects Ravindra Saste Shrushti Hitech and Agriculture Development was established by Mr. Ravindra     Saste in 2001. Since its inception it develops green houses all over India. Most of Shrushti’s green houses are developed for farmers of Maharashtra, M.P, and Gujarat. Farmers of these regions are more inclined for floriculture, when compared to other farmers across the country. Located at Baramati in Pune district in Maharastra. Green houses for roses is what Shrushti Hitech specialises in. “Gerbera and Carnation flower crops yield good production and are more profitable” adds Mr. Saste. He further adds that development of green house farming, is a positive change that India has witnessed in the recent years. In the future, Shrushti Hitech hopes to develop a new technology in greenhouse to prevent plants getting affected by high temperature and wind speed.  Mr. Ravindra Saste is a B.Sc degree holder. He has been associated with this field for 12 years. He believes this field endures success and profit through honest work.
