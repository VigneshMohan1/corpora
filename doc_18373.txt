The numerous educational institutes and colleges in and around the city have earned it the title of the 'Cradle of Education' of Karnataka. Easily accessed by all means of transport, the educational hub is visited by young people from different corners. Also, people for business as well as family trips visit Mangalore in huge number. From the wide array of lodging options, one can either prefer an affordable accommodation that provides basic facilities, or if one is ready to splurge, he/she can go for a luxurious hotel and enjoy all the deluxe services and facilities, such as day-and-night room service, swimming pool, spa, games and sports, health club, etc. However, if one wants a home away from home, homestay is the apt choice. There are many well-to-do homestay options available in the city at reasonable charges.
