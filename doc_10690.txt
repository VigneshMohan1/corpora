In standard construction, the SAB machines are delivered with the following features. The machine is fitted with inlet with feed roll. The aspiration chamber is fitted with one waste auger. The screen system consists of one sieveboat with 1 short scalping screen layer (1 screen part) and 2 long grading/sand screen layers (2 screen parts).
