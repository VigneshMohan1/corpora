Keywords:  extension education, farming, dairy, mushroom, bee-keeping, horticulture, Issue Date:  2015 Publisher:  Directorate of Extension Education, Bihar Agricultural University, Sabour. Abstract:  The book “Agri-entrepreneurs of Bihar” is a collection of successful cases of farmers who have transformed agriculture into a mega-entrepreneurial activity in Bihar. The cases are from all the twenty Krishi Vigyan Kendras located in different districts of Bihar under the jurisdiction of Bihar Agricultural University, Sabour. The cases in several domains of agriculture and allied disciplines underline the importance of agriculture in the state which has the capability to bring about a change in the entrepreneurial potential of the state.
