Using this data for producing superior germ plasm. Preservation of indigenous germ plasm. Milk recording of cattle and buffaloes as the first step for improving dairy cattle milk production. Implementing Agencies: CHRS Unit Rohtak in Haryana CHRS Unit Ahmedabad in Gujarat CHRS Unit Ajmer in Rajasthan CHRS Unit Ongole in Andhra Pradesh Target Group/Beneficiaries: The primary aim is to identify elite germ plasm for further improvement in milk production. Indigenous breeds covered at present are Gir, Kankrej, Hariana, Ongole of cattle and Murrah, Jaffrabadi, Surti and Mehsani of buffaloes. Under the scheme incentives for rearing of elite cows, buffaloes and their male calves is given to the owners of the elite animals.   Funding pattern: 100% Central Grant.   Targets and Achievements: Financial progress of Central Herd Registration Scheme, Ongole(A.P)                      since 2007-08 to 2012-13(Up to September 2012)   Year Allocation Expenditure (Rs in lakhs) 2007-08 25.95 25.72 2008-09 38.08 37.15 2009-10 38.10 37.66 2010-11 38.33 38.26 2011-12 52.69 52.59 2012-13 (up to September 2012) 62.59 25.89   Physical achievements :- Targets and achievement for Central Herd Registration Scheme , Ongole (A.P) since 2005-06 to 2012-13 (Up to September 2012) Year Primary Registration Final Registration Target Achievement Target Achievement 2005-06 1200 1293 700 633 2006-07 1400 1227 800 881 2007-08 1400 1331 800 809 2008-09 1400 1305 800 832 2009-10 1400 1232 800 874 2010-11 1400 1539 800 1000 2011-12 1400 1539 800 863 2012-13 (up to September 2012) 1400 937 800 895   For further details please contact:                                                                                                   Assistant Registrar, Central Herd Registration Scheme, H.
