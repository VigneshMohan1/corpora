The place has been named Subhash Chandra Bose, the famous freedom fighter had spent a lot of time in the region in the year 1937. Subhash Badoli is a charismatic spot circumscribed by huge towering trees. The natural spectacle of the place makes is one of the most popular tourist spots in Dalhousie. Subhash Badoli is famous for the captivating view of the snow capped peaks that it offers. It is here that Subhash Chandra Bose sat and meditated during his stay at Dalhousie. The same historical location is now provided with seating arrangements where tourists can sit and enjoy the natural panorama of the overlooking mountains which gives a canvas like feeling. The majestic mountains and the canopy of the blue sky reverberates the memory of the great hero of Indian independence.
