Even Prosopis and Eucalyptus are also used for promoting timber after proper seasoning. Colour of the wood is also changed by Ammonia fumigation. The experiments resulted in low cost furniture and durable building materials which will promote rural entrepreneurship. The following experiments have been conducted under Forest Utilization Division UTILIZATION OF TIMBER SPECIES AS BIRD’S NEST Bird’s nests have been made from Eucalyptus tereticornis, Eucalyptus grandis, Pinus patula, Kaya senegalensis and Eucalyptus alba etc. TREE GUARDS MADE FROM DIFFERENT AGRO FORESTRY SPECIES Tree guards have been made from Eucalyptus tereticornis, Eucalyptus grandis, Acacia mangium, Acacia auriculiformis and Pinus patula etc FABRICATION OF FURNITURE’S FOR SCHOOL CHILDREN   Tables and chairs for tribal school children have been made from Eucalyptus tereticornis, Eucalyptus grandis, Pinus patual, Acacia auriculiformis, Chloroxylon swietenia etc RESEARCH ON PLYWOOD, MATCHWOOD AND VENEER TREES To develop package of practice for cultivation of matchwood, plywood and veneer tree using all modern inputs by adopting improved technique and thereby increasing the supply to meet out the demand.
