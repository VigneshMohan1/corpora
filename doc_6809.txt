Accordingly, a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, 'National Food Security Mission' (NFSM), has been launched from 2007-08 to operationalise the above mentioned resolution. Mission Objectives: Increasing production of Rice, Wheat, Pulses through area expansion and productivity enhancement in a sustainable manner in the identified districts of the country.
