Go to the shop Sri Lakshmivenkateshwara Nursery Home Sri Lakshmivenkateshwara Nursery Quits corporate life and starts rose nursery on NH 207, Tamil Nadu Kishore M.G Sri Lakshmivenkateshwara Nursery situated on National Highway 207 – Sarjapur to Bagalur Main Road in Tamil Nadu is spread into 20,000 sq. ft. Mr. Kishore M.G., is the proprietor of the Nursery. All varieties of Rose are grown with main focus on Dutch Rose. These are developed to be planted under green house and also in open areas. Earlier Mr. Kishore was working in a multi national company. He has left the job due to odd working hours and high work stress. Later he decided to grow rose plants in his dry land influenced by the people near his land growing the same. Then the decision made was fruitful, he runs the nursery successfully today. Initial Ordeals : First, people in the area let him down. Second, he did not know when to fill the cover and do budding. Third, when to put the pesticides to plants and fourth, the most crucial, not clear when to send the plants for marketing. After going through rough patch, Mr. Kishore then started studying plants particularly rose plants and few indoor plants, finally got through tough times. Target Customers : People who plan to start working on green house for the first time and the farmers who grow roses in open area are main target customers. Mr. Kishor receives new orders through reference of existing customers as they are satisfied with plant’s quality and end output. Rose Plants Farming : One full cycle takes around 45 -60 days as first the plants are planted into small bags (size 3×4) and after about 25-35 days the roots grow. Next step is to remove the thorns from the plant and leave it for a week or so and then proceed with budding of the plants. After about 30-35 days the plants will be ready to be planted in the greenhouse and in case it to be planted in the open area, the same rose plant is again repacked into a bigger bag (5×7) and then grown for few days making it strong enough to withstand the problems caused like heat or other things in an open area. Precautions : Fungicide and other medicines are sprayed at least 2 times a week and plants are kept clean. Plants are watered everyday. Mr. Kishore M.G., is a B. Sc., graduate from Bangalore University. He has worked in many industries such as BPO, multi national companies before plunging into garden cultivation. Mr. Kishore says, “if we maintain quality of our plants, there is no need to worry about the number of companies or nurseries that crop up which could be competitors for us.’’ “Our vision in the next five years is to cultivate all types of rose plants along with all types of indoor and outdoor plants,’’ shares Mr. Kishore.
