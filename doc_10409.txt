Your shopping bag is empty. Go to the shop Jai Solar Systems Home Jai Solar Systems Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu based manufacturers and suppliers of solar driven products. Also offers solar fencing Antony David Rajnikant Jai Solar Systems was established in 2003. It is located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. Mr. Antony David Rajnikant is the partner. The company’s main thrust is on solar fencing and it charges Rs.1.75 lakhs per km. Its annual turnover is around Rs. 2 crore. Jai Solar Systems has developed a core competence in the field of manufacturing of solar water heater, solar street lights, solar fencing, solar UPS and solar home lighting. It sustained growth since its inception on the basis of unmatched quality and customer satisfaction. The company has got a well equipped in-house production unit with state-of-the-art technology and hi-tech machinery. Research and development team is continuously making efforts to make the products highly innovative. Mr. Antony David is an MBA in Marketing and has got 10 years of experience in solar field. He says “We are trying to educate farmers by conducting exhibitions and road surveys. We go to farmers and tell them about the solar fencing and its benefits. It can be made affordable by using ordinary wood for fencing. Farmers should decide what is important for them. To protect the crops from animal, farmers have no other way than to do fencing for their lands. Nowadays farmers are showing keen interest in solar fencing.’’ “We are getting more response from the public regarding solar systems. Now we are also going into the solar power plant. We are putting lot of solar power plants for general public. In the next five years we want to produce more electricity via solar. We are diversifying into solar power plants,’’ adds Mr. Antony David. “Nationalized banks are giving loans for solar fencing at 5 percent interest. Clients are satisfied by our quality products. We also strive to keep a high profile,” says Mr. Antony David.
