Armed with world class hotels, Coorg promises the "quality time" that one looks forward to spend with oneself, with an added advantage of lush green surroundings, coffee plantations and ancient bungalows. The diverse range of hotels in Coorg that has 5 Star hotels on one end and budget hotels or home stays on the other makes it an ideal destination for both vacationers and business travellers. Owing to the spending capacity, the hospitality and services of your select hotel will be at par with your needs and requirements, thus promising utmost comfort and pleasure. Traditional decor skillfully blended with latest technology is an unmissable aspect of every hotel in Coorg. Some important hotels of Coorg are Hotel Coorg International, Orange County Resort, Club Mahindra Kodagu Valley, Hotel Heritage Resort, Leisure Vacations Woodstock Villas, Coorg Jungle Camp holiday Resort, Sri Venkateshwara Residency, Amanvana Spa Resort, Vivanta by Taj Madikeri, Capitol Village Resort Madikeri, Club Mahindra Virajpet etc. The special features of these hotels include spacious well furnished rooms particularly equipped with modern day conveniences like satellite TV, intercom and Wi-Fi systems. In house restaurants serving delectable multiple cuisines promise a fine dining experience. Also, the presence of several budget accommodations and home stays in Coorg makes it easy for vacationers travelling on a budget. Despite being economical, they are furnished with necessary amenities required to ensure a comfy stay.
