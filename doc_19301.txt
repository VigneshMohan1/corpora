The subtropical forests eco-region covers the state along with its mountain forests and the lowland tropical forests to the north and south. The forests are prominent for their biodiversity of different mammals, birds, and flora. Wildlife Sanctuaries & National Parks in Meghalaya There are two National Parks and two wildlife sanctuaries in Meghalaya. Balpakram National Park Location: It is located around 167 km away from Tura, district headquarter of West Garo Hills Nokrek Biosphere Reserve Location: It is located 2 km from Tura Peak in the Garo Hills district.
