Details Directorate General of Commercial Intelligence and Statistics (DGCI&S), Ministry of Commerce & Industry, has been mandated to develop a comprehensive database on India's services sector trade. DGCI&S has engaged ICRIER, a New-Delhi based think tank to conduct on-line survey of companies for select category of services. This data will provide critical inputs to the Department of Commerce for policy formulation, including designing of appropriate benefit schemes for our much valued exporters. This data will also be used to compile a database of companies who will be invited for government meeting related to policy formulation, export incentives and trade negotiations among others. The links to the on-line survey questionnaires for four sectors namely telecommunication services, audio-visual services, logistics services and professional services are given below. Service providers in these sectors are requested to register and fill up the questionnaire. This will enable the government to create a business directory for providing benefits. You can fill the questionnaire online or can download the offline form and upload it after duly filling the information. All information will be treated as confidential .
