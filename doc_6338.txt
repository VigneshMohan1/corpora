Upcoming events… Contact Us OSU LID Researchers and other Contacts Login Info Oklahoma Low Impact Development Oklahoma Low Impact Development — filed under: News     Low Impact Development (LID) is a new, comprehensive land planning and engineering design approach with a goal of maintaining and enhancing the pre-development hydrologic regime of urban and developed watersheds.  The Oklahoma Cooperative Extension Service has a goal of providing information and design aids related to low impact development that will make an impact on stormwater management in Oklahoma.  This website is continually evolving, so feel free to email Jason Vogel , Stormwater Specialist in the Biosystems and Agricultural Engineering Department, with your comments or suggestions.  Thanks!
