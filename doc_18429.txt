The place is visited by a large number of tourists every year from India as well as overseas to glance at the becharming natural scenery. Also, it is a haven for honeymooners. The beauteous luster of the lake and the lofty hills gilded with lush tea gardens adds zing to the romantic holiday of the couples in Ooty. The small town, also known as Scotland of the East, offers numerous options of accommodation to its guests, be it a low-priced lodge, mid-range hotel or an expensive deluxe resort. However, taking in account the changing weather of the place, and if money is not a constraint, then tourists must opt for a hotel that facilitates a full array of suitable services, including central heating, 24 hours running hot water, laundry, health club, spa, bonfire, and certain other additional conveniences.
