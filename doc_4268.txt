They say leaders can not be created; leaders are born. True they are in every sense. However, one can surely create the professionals who know the industry details, have the proper insight about the business methods and can create a mark in the success of an organization by their performances. Business schools in India are exactly in this mission. Top Indian business schools consistently produce management professionals who don't only play major roles in the success of Indian Inc; they also have their hands behind the success of overseas organizations across the globe. The list of business schools in India is dominated by the Indian Institute of Management (IIM). Fully autonomous institute owned by the Central Government of India, IIM has its centers located in various parts across the country including Ahmedabad, Kolkata (Calcutta), Bangalore, Kozhikode, Indore, Lucknow and Shillong. Besides the IIMs, there are also other major B-Schools in India like XLRI, Faculty of Management Studies, Jamnalal Bajaj Institute of Management Studies, Xavier Institute of Management and many more.
