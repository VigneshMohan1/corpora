November 2014 Symposium on Combating Wildlife Crime: Securing Enforcement, Ensuring Justice, and Upholding the Rule of Law - The Proceedings Illegal wildlife trade is a growing global phenomenon that has serious ecological, economic, security, and social costs and often involves highly organized crime groups who exploit wild fauna and flora at unsustainable levels. October 2014 Sustainable Vocational Training toward Industrial Upgrading and Economic Transformation This report summarizes results of the “Sustainable Vocational Training toward Industrial Upgrading and Economic Transformation” workshop held on 2-5 December 2013 in Beijing and Guangzhou, the People’s Republic of China. September 2014 中国城镇化背景下的 公私合作伙伴关系 研讨会 本报告汇总了2013年8月22-23日在北京召开的城镇化背景下公私合作伙伴关系（以下简称“PPP”） 研讨会的会议记录。该研讨会由财政部、国家发展与改革委员会、亚行、财政科学研究所PPP研究 委员会和亚行区域知识共享中心组织。 来自中央政府、35个地方政府、金融机构、私营服务提供 商、学术和研究机构以及发展伙伴的约200名人员参加了本次研讨会。研讨会上，从事PPP规划、准 备和实施的与会者分享了他们在PPP领域的知识和最佳实践方法。 该研讨会由财政部、国家发展与改革委员会、亚行、财政部财政科学研究所PPP研究专业委员会和 亚行区域知识共享中心共同举办。 September 2014 Public-Private Partnerships in Urbanization in the People's Republic of China This report summarizes the proceedings of the Public–Private Partnerships (PPP) in Urbanization workshop held in Beijing on 22–23 August 2013. September 2014 Third ASEAN Chief Justices’ Roundtable on Environment: ASEAN’s Environmental Challenges and Legal Responses – The Proceedings This publication contains the proceedings of the Third ASEAN Chief Justices’ Roundtable on Environment: ASEAN’s Environmental Challenges and Legal Responses, held in Bangkok on 15-18 November 2013. September 2014 Recent Trends in Road Asset Management and Case Studies: Road Asset Management Workshop Proceedings This document contains the workshop proceedings from the Transport Community of Practice Workshop on Road Asset Management held in ADB headquarters. This includes summaries for each session, case studies tackled, and key conclusions/actions. July 2014 RETA 7834: Assessment and Implications of Rationalizing and Phasing Out Fossil Fuel Subsidies: Finalization Conference Report This publication presents the preliminary results of an ADB technical assistance study on fossil fuel subsidy reform in India, Indonesia, and Thailand. July 2014 Summary of Proceedings of the 47th Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors This publication summarizes the proceedings of the 47th Annual Meeting of the ADB Board of Governors, held in Astana, Kazakhstan from 2 to 5 May 2014.
