TAKE OUT THE PULR OF RIPE MANGO. 2. TAKE A JUICER JAR AND TRANSFER THIS MANGO PULP INTO IT. 3.NOW ADD CURD,SUGAR,ELACHI POWDER AND ICE CUBES AND BLEND IT TILL SMOOTH. 4. NOW TRANFER THIS LASSI INTO A BOWL AND GARNISH WITH SAFFRON. 5.SERVE CHILLED.
