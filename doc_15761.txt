Chandrababu Naidu is the current Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh. After resignation of the previous Chief Minister N. Kiran Kumar Reddy in March 2014, the state was under President's rule. The new state of Telangana came into existence on 2 June 2014 with K Chandrasekhar Rao as its chief minister.
