Home » Agra » Bageshwarnath Temple Bageshwarnath Temple Bageshwarnath Temple is a very popular religious spot in the vicinity of Raja Ki Mandi. Located in the Civil Lines, one of the most posh area of Agra, Bageshwarnath Temple is about 2 km away from the city center. Bageshwarnath Temple is located off the Ramratan Road in the northern region of Agra, Uttar pradesh . St. John's College and the Sales Tax Office are the important offices in the area. The Bageshwarnath Temple is one of those religious spots in Agra that attract a huge number of tourists and devotees every year. Apart from being a major religious attraction the temple also serves as an important landmark of the area.
