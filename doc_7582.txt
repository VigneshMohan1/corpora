Till date, India holds the third position in the competition. Gold medal As a team, India has won gold in the following categories: Women's 10 meter Air Pistol In the individual category, Navdeep Singh Rathore has won gold medal in the Men's 50 m Rifle Prone category. Abhinav Bindra has won gold medal in the Men's 10 m Air Rifle category. Raj Kumari has won gold medal in the Women's 50 m Rifle Prone category. Abhinav Bindra , who also won a gold medal in the Beijing Olympic Games 2008, did not miss his target in spite of a bout of fever on Friday, January 13, 2012. He defeated Zhu Qinan of China, a gold medalist in the Asian Games, in the Men's 10 m Air Rifle category by a mere margin of 0.3 points. This was his first gold medal in this championship. Silver Medal As a team, India has won silver in the following categories: Men's 10 m Air Rifle Women's 10 m Air Rifle (Junior) Women's 10 m Air Rifle (Senior) Women's 50 m Rifle Prone (Junior) Men's 50 m Pistol (Junior) Bronze Medal As a team, India has won bronze in the following categories: Men's 50 m Rifle Prone (Junior) Men's 10 m Air Rifle (Junior) Men's 10 m Air Pistol (Junior) Women's 25 m Pistol (Junior) Women's 10 m Air Pistol (Junior) Men's Double Trap (Senior) H. Sanahal Singh has won the bronze medal in the Men's 50 m Rifle Prone category. In the Women's 10 m Air Pistol category, Annu Raj Singh has won the bronze medal.
