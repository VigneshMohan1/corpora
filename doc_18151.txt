Vimala Thakur is an eminent religious philosopher and social reformer. The Ashram lies between Subhash Chowk and Gandhi Chowk and located on the Mall Road of the town. The Shivkul Ashram in Dalhousie is nestled in the lap of the beautiful Pir-Pinjal Hills that connects the Chamba district with the neighboring state of Jammu and Kashmir. Vimala Bahen organizes lecture sessions in Dalhousie during the summer seasons. The Shivkul is the venue during these lecture sessions on spiritualism and disciples from all over the world come to attend them.
