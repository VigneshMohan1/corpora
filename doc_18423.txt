Here, you can feel the essence of the royal past in imperial palace-turned hotels in the city. Hotels like Hotel Udaigarh will compel you to spend more days in the beautiful city. It overlooks the groovy blue waters of the gorgeous Pichola Lake, and the Aravalli mountain ranges. The balconies protruding from traditionally designed regal chambers of the palace-turned hotel grant a look of the endearing city. However, an encounter with the �Regal Rajasthan� is possible even if you want to spend less. The budget hotels in the city also have traditionally built interiors with all basic facilities. Moreover, to attract more number of tourists to their hotels, owners have started services like mehandi for women visitors, Rajasthani attire for men and women, in-house restaurants serving ethnic food of the region.
