The state of Uttar Pradesh has a good number of medical colleges and institutes that impart quality medical education to the students of medical science. The medical colleges are mainly situated in the major towns and cities of Uttar Pradesh. Medical Courses Offered The medical courses at undergraduate and post-graduate level, offered by the medical colleges in Uttar Pradesh , are summed up below. Under-Graduate Medical Courses M.B.B.S (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery) B. D. S. (Bachelor of Dental Sciences) B. H. M. S. (Bachelor of Homoeopathic Medicine & Surgery) B.A.M.S B. Pharm. (Bachelor of Pharmacy) B. P. T. (Bachelor of Physiotherapy) B. M. L. T. (Bachelor of Medical Laboratory Technology) B.U.M.S Post-Graduate Medical Courses M.D. (Doctor of Medicine) M.S. (Master of Surgery) Postgraduate Diploma in medicine Apart from these, several other specialty, diploma, certificate and doctoral medical programs are also available. Admission Procedure It is compulsory for the aspirants of Uttar Pradesh to seat for the competitive entrance test before they can enroll themselves in the medical colleges of Uttar Pradesh. More over, the students should also have completed their 12th standard with science and must have secured a minimum of 50% marks.
