Maharashtra is well known among the students of India, for its higher education system. The state houses some of the finest and reputed medical colleges of India. These medical colleges are spread all over Maharashtra, most of them being situated in the important districts of Maharashtra like Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur, etc. Every year the medical colleges of Maharashtra produce top class medical professionals and scholars. With state of the art educational facilities and eminent faculty, the medical colleges of Maharashtra are among the most sought after colleges. The state has a good number of Government as well as private medical institutes. Maharashtra medical institutions provides the students with the opportunity to select from a wide range of programs like MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS, B.Pharmacy, MD, MS, M.Pharmacy to name a few. Speciality, certificate, diploma and doctoral programs on medical science can also be pursued at the medical colleges of Maharashtra. Admission Procedure If you are an aspirant of medical education and wish to enroll yourself in the medical colleges of Maharashtra, then like the other states, in Madhya Pradesh also you have to seat for the Common Entrance Test (CET). Prior to this, in order to make yourself eligible to seat for the test, you have to secure 50% marks in 10+2 examination with relevant combination of Science subjects. Admission is based strictly on the performance in CET. Admission to medical programs at post graduate level can be pursued after finishing MBBS or equivalent degree.
