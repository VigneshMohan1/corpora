It hosts one of the largest wildlife sanctuaries of India, the Periyar National Park. Domestic as well as international tourists visit Thekkady to live amidst the serene atmosphere and explore the bountiful treasures of the green world. Surrounded by sprawling green gardens, the hotel with its top-drawer facilities and outstanding services assures that you savor absolute relaxation during your stay. The rooms in this hotel have been categorised as Gold, Platinum and Diamond, tourists can choose a room as per their convenience. The hotel facilitates some star services like helipad, a lounge bar, a rooftop restaurant, a health club, and a spa.
