Chetna was chosen by the State Level Nodal Agency to implement the program in 4500 hectares in Dandepally Mandal, Adilabad District to achieve the following outcomes; Prevention of soil run-off, regeneration of natural vegetation, rain water harvesting and recharging of the ground water table. Increase in cropping intensity and agricultural/animal husbandry productivity Increase in income of farmers/ landless laborers in the project area Creation/enhancement of livelihoods for the asset-less and active involvement of SHGs for building financial resources to attain self sustainability Integration with other schemes/programs through convergence for achieving overall development of the villages Updates Here are a few images from the recent elections to the watershed committees in Dandepally. Chetna Organic is implementing the Integrated Watershed Management Programme with support from the Central and State Government under the District Watershed Management Agency (DWMA), Adilabad District in Dandepalli Mandal. The project is being implemented in a total of 4 Gram Panchayats. As part of the programme,many activities like Gramasabhas, Kalajathas, Film Shows, Awarenes Meetings, Socio Economic survey and Participatory Net Planning have already been undertaken. Recently, user groups were formed in five Micro Watersheds  and elections were held to appoint the 15 Village Watershed committees members In Mamidipalli Micro Watershed  34 User Groups were formed. With two representatives from each group a total 68 members are selected to the Village Watershed Society. These 68 members in turn selected 15 members to the Watershed Executive Committee.
