As per the 2011 Census, Chhattisgarh has a total population of 25,540,196; previously it was 20,833,803 in 2001. Out of the total population of Chhattisgarh, the male population is 12,827,915 and the female population is 12,712,281. The total population growth was 22.59 percent since the last census and the population of the state forms 2.11 percent of the total population in India. The total area of Chhattisgarh is 135,191 sq. km with a Density of 189 per sq km. The Sex Ratio in Chhattisgarh is 991 for each 1000 male. In the last census of 2001, the sex ratio was 990 per 1000 males in Chhattisgarh. The child sex ratio is 964 per 1000 males and has increased as compared to 868 in the 2001 census. The Literacy rate in Chhattisgarh has risen and is 71.04 percent, with male literacy at 81.45 percent and female literacy at 60.59 percent. In actual figures, total literates in Chhattisgarh are 15,598,314 out of which males are 8,962,121 and females are 6,636,193.
