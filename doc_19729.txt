Cooperative Extension Service Weather The Oklahoma Cooperative Extension Service develops programs based on science-based, objective information to help Oklahomans solve problems, promote leadership, and manage resources wisely. Below are websites that provide information about Weather. Weather Oklahoma Mesonet - The Oklahoma Mesonet is Oklahoma's world-class network of environmental monitoring stations. Ag Weather - Welcome to Agweather, a website designed to help agricultural producers with their weather-related decisions. Disaster Education & Assistance Team - The Oklahoma Cooperative Extension Service (OCES) Disaster Education and Assistance Team (DEAT) provides coordination for disaster education programs for extension audiences; organizes training programs for OCES professionals; and serves as a point of contact for outside agencies. OK-FIRE - OK-FIRE provides weather-based decision support products for wildland fire management in Oklahoma.
