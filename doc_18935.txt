There are three international airports in Kerala, at Trivandrum (Thiruvananthapuram), Kozhikode and Cochin. The Thiruvananthapuram International Airport and the Nedumbasherry International Airport at Cochin operate various domestic and international flights thereby connecting Kerala to different parts of India and the world. Thiruvananthapuram, the capital of Kerala is connected to the important cities of India including, New-Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, and Mumbai. How to reach Kerala state by airways from these major Indian cities- It takes nearly three hours to reach the capital from Mumbai, Around four hours from New-Delhi and an hour from Chennai .Since there are no direct flights available from Kolkata to Thiruvananthapuram the quickest way to reach Kerala is to board a flight bound for Chennai from Kolkata and then take a flight to the capital of Kerala. You will be there in three and a half hours There are regular flights from Thiruvananthapuram to Singapore, Colombo, Male, Muscat, Bahrain, Kuwait, Abu Dhabi and Dubai. International Airlines like Gulf Air, Qatar Airways, Singapore Airlines, Air Lanka, Air Maldives and Air India operate many flights from Thiruvananthapuram. Domestic Carriers like Kingfisher Airlines, Air-Deccan, and Indian Airlines, Jet Airways to name a few fly in and out of Kerala. Airports are located on the outskirts of the city. The Thiruvananthapuram International Airport lies at the southern end of Kerala while the international airport at Cochin lies between Cochin and Thrissur, 35kms from Ernakulam.The airport at Calicut is located at a distance of 26kms from Calicut Railway Station and is situated in the northern part of Kerala. Most airports have prepaid taxi services on offer. How to reach Kerala is a very important query since Kerala is one of the most sought after destinations in the world. With many private and public airlines operating kelrala flights air travel previously a rich man's privilege is well within the common man's reach. You can even avail various holiday packages to Kerala at discount.
