Located in the God's own country, Munnar is a well-known honeymoon destination. Alike other hill stations in India, Munnar also has many hotels and resorts for the visitors, ranging from ordinary to plush lodging options. However, a holiday in one of the most evocative town should be something more than just a visit, one must live the moments. Thereby, the place has numerous homestay options for its tourists, to offer you a tranquil stay with homelike warmth. Also, you will get multiple backpacker-type accommodations in the town if you are out on a short thrill-seeking trip in Munnar. The town offers its charm the most during the winter months, i.e. from November to February. Tourists in huge number visit the town to sail above the clouds across the colossal mountain ranges.
