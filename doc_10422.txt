Go to the shop Krishidhan Seeds opens ‘24X7 toll free customer help line center’ Home Krishidhan Seeds opens ‘24X7 toll free customer help line center’ Krishidhan Seeds starts ‘24X7 toll free customer help line center’, to solve farmers’ problems in a short time on on October 30, 2012 in Bangalore. The center shall provide technical know how as well as guide farmers in every step of the farming process. It is free of charge and it’s services shall be available to farmers 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Through the opening of the help line, Krishidhan Seeds aims to provide quick and reliable solutions to farmers at any given time. Admirably, the efforts do not cease there. Armed with a highly skilled and qualified team of individuals, Krishidhan Seeds regularly sends over 150 agricultural graduates and company trained professionals to visit farming villages. These individuals see every farmer in need so that they may share their technical and methodical knowledge in a high quality and apt manner. All areas of expertise are covered during these visits like guiding them about their soil type, moisture it contains, crops which will be more viable according to soil type to harvest duration of the crop. This timely guidance by industry experts helps farmer work more aptly. Further, the farmers shall be having daily commodity price details at their doorstep with the help of Krishidhan Seeds and Multi Commodity Exchange. In 2011 when Krishidhan entered into a partnership with the Multi Commodity Exchange (MCX) to deliver live information on an array of commodity prices to farmers. Through this endeavor, farmers have been able to gain knowledge on the frequent fluctuations of market prices and, in turn, have been able to develop sharper and more effective marketing strategies.
