Your shopping bag is empty. Go to the shop RK Solar Agencies Home RK Solar Agencies Suppliers of solar based motors and fences RK Solar Agencies was established in 2009 by Mr. Ramakrishna Reddy. This Andhra Pradesh-based company provides solar water pumps and solar fences for agricultural purpose. The turnover of the company ranges from Rs. 80 lakhs to 1 crore.  Solar motors enable bore well motors to run without power, while solar fences provide security to farms. These products can be operated with or without battery depending on its type. The average life span of these products extends to 10 years. The company provides a guarantee of two years from the date of purchase. Agricultural farmers are the target customers of the company. Their products are mainly sold in Chittoor and Anantapur in Andhra Pradesh. Service staffs of RK Solar Agencies visit customer locations every 3 months for maintenance-related activities. Sales staffs that visit different locations regularly ensure easy availability of their products. All products are MNRE approved. Premium products designed for both corporate and common men are available with the company. “More features and low prices are the reasons why our products are in demand in the market,” shares Mr. Reddy. The company plans to focus on artificial intelligence in the future. Mr. Reddy holds a B.Sc degree. He has 8 years of experience in the solar industry.
