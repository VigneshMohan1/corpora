The total out lay for Horticulture Department is Rs.72.765 crores. As a first phase in 2007-08, the project was implemented in 9 sub-basins Varahanadhi, Upper Vellar, South Vellar, Pambar, Manimuthar, Kottakkaraiyar, Arjunanadhi, Palar, Aliyar  and an area of 6047 Ha horticultural crops has been introduced against the target of 6139 ha. with an expenditure of 634.77 lakhs. In the Second Phase, the Project was extended to another 16 sub-basins viz, Pennaiyar, Swethanadhi, Anaivari Odai, Chinnar, Agniar, Ambuliyar, Upper Vaigai, Varattar-Nagalar, Upper Gundar, Therkar, Senkottaiyar, Sindapalli-Uppodai, Nishabanadhi, Kalinagalar, Poiney, Koundinyanadhi and an of 9310 Ha has been covered out of the Target of 9564 ha. A sum of Rs. 1553.160 lakhs has been incurred as expenditure. During 2010-11 the Project was extended to another 30 viz, Araniyar, Kosasthalaiyar, Ongur, Nallavur, Thurinjalar, Pambar to thirukoilur, gadilam, Markandanadhi, Kambainallur, Pambanar-Varattar, Gomukinadhi, Theniar, Girdhamal, Kanal Odai, Lower Gundar, Vembar, Uthirakosamangai, Palar, Sevalaperiyar, Deviar, Nagarier, Vallampatti,  Uppathur, Kovilar, Uppodai, Salikulamaru, Korampallam, Karumeniyar, Vaipar, Hannumannadhi  . During 2010-11 for the Fourth Phase the Project was extended to another 5 sub basins viz, Kayalkudiyar, Cheyyar and Kiliyar, Paraliyar, Cooum and Adayar and IV-2 Phase the project was extended Amaravathi sub basin.  During 2010-11 for the Fourth year of first phase for the third year second phase and first year third phase an area expansion of 8152 ha has been covered out of the Target of 9377 ha.  A sum of Rs. 1283.27 lakhs has been incurred as expenditure.  During 2011-12 for the fifth year of First phase, second year of Third phase and first year of Fourth phase an area expansion of 8182 ha is programmed with an outlay of Rs. 1338.45 lakhs.  SI.No Year Physical (Ha) Financial(Rs in Lakhs) 1 2007-08 6139 6047 659.06 637.77 2 2008-09 9564 9310 1621.39 1553.16 3 2009-10 4891 4744 871.07 783.50 4 2010-11 9377 8152 1710.61 1283.27 5 2011-12 8182 6562 1338.45 1005.60   Total 38513 34815 6200.58 5263.30    Visitor Counter: 843527 Since: 15th October 2012 Accessibility Options Terms & Conditions Disclaimer Accessibility Statement Sitemap Hyperlinking Policy FAQs Feedback Privacy Policy Copyright Policy Copyright © Department of Horticulture and Plantation Crops.
