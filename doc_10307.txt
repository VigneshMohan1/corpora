Located at New Delhi Chandel Agritech Solutions was set up in 2007 at New Delhi by Mr. Manoj Singh and Ms. Babita. The company provides Agro Produce Marketing Advisory and Consulting Services to clients based upon their requirement. It is involved in strengthening supply chain management in agriculture and allied sectors. Mr. Manoj Singh holds masters in horticulture and has management background. He says, “Horticulture is basically growing of fruits, flowers vegetables, ornamental flowers etc. Presently it is a flourishing industry now and contributes 30% of agriculture GDP in India.” He further adds, “horticultural products being fresh commodities have limited shelf life hence needs immediate marketing. It is crucial one should know about the marketing needs. There are many factors involved in the marketing of Horticultural products.” Marketing Channels : The horticultural produce are sold to APMC and marketed through the traders and retailers. The appropriate marketing channels should be selected depending on the commodities. Target Market : Farmer must decide his market and then decide on marketing alternatives. If products are to be sold in local market, bigger high end market, retail market or international market. Harvesting : The time taken for commutation from the farm till the market must also be considered when harvesting the crop. Pre harvest and post harvest management practices have to be implemented meticulously. Packaging : All the fresh produce has to be packed appropriately as per the transit mode, period and location. It is important to strengthen supply chain at every aspect right from farming till end sale in order to have smooth operations across the process and making it profitable deal. This is where CAS plays its role by offering supply chain management based solutions in agribusiness,” concludes Mr. Singh. Our Correspondent Contact Details-Chandel Agritech Solutions Pvt.
