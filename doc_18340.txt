If you are out on a holiday in Dalhousie, the Switzerland of India, then you are sure to enjoy a pleasant stay amidst mesmerizing scenic landscape. There are several budget hotels, guest houses, luxury hotels as well as resorts to accommodate visitors from across the world. Besides, visitors can opt for independent villas and bungalows. A holiday in Dalhousie may not cost heavy on your pocket, if you prefer a budget hotel with basic facilities and comfortable rooms; however, for availing special facilities like a cable TV, centrally heated rooms, internet access, 24*7 room service and travel desk, you should go for a resort or a deluxe hotel. In certain hotels, there are provisions for holding family functions and business meetings as well. Grand View Hotel, situated in the heart of Dalhousie, is one of the largest hotels in the town. To suit different income groups, the hotel has categorised its rooms and suites into three kinds: Economy, Luxury and Imperial. The Terrace Lawn and the Observation Deck at the hotel have been designed so as to get you a trancing view, and let you unwind all your stresses and discomforts. Moreover, there is a coffee shop, recreation centre, wellness centre, and much more within the premises.
