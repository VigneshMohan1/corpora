The elders find in here the fuel for the soul, while the young generation revel the adventurous fun activities. Every year thousands of people head towards Mussoorie for rejuvenation in the foothills of the Himalayas. Mussoorie, a summer retreat for the people of north Inida, takes care of its guest by offering them a plethora of choices of accommodation, ranging from cost-effective lodges to high-end resorts. Usually, hotels with distinct style, top-drawer services and incredible location, i.e., overlooking the spectacular Doon Valley, are the ones which are best-loved by the tourists. Also, the place hosts few heritage hotels, such as Padmini Nivas, Kasmanda Palace, etc. However, tourists who have a special interest in shopping can book a room in a hotel situated along the famous Mall Road of the town, so that one can go shopping as and when he/she wishes to.
