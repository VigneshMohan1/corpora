Response will nevertheless be in the language of the State. Gradually all regional languages will be enabled for pull requests also. In case of 7738299899, Pull SMS will be charged as per the rental or special-pack plan of the subscriber. Users will be charged as per the following table (rates applicable as on date) for the messages sent to 51969.
