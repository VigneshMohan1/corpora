It is advisable to start navigating the site by first log-in as suggested below : If you have been assigned access thru username and password, please click on Login tab available on the top banner on the site and provide the said details in the boxes shown thereafter, as shown below: If you have been assigned access thru IP access, you just require to click at "IP Login", shown below the username and password boxes on the top banner of the site, as shown below : The site can be navigated for data either after making a Log in the site or even without Login. For navigation , please follow following steps : Whether you are logged in or not, click on any of the available 34 main data categories presented under "Socio-Economic Statistical Information about India" shown on the site's homepage. Click on any of the main data category will leads to a page where the alphabetical list of its sub-categories will be shown to you. Clicking a sub-category will lead you to a further sub-category or to the list of data table headings pertaining to the sub-category clicked. Above the listing of sub-categories, the listing of main data categories is available for the purpose of direct navigating to them from the page. Click on any of the presented data sub-category will fetch you the list of data tables available for the data sub-category. The page showing data table heading shows you data at India level or State-wise or Region-wise consolidated data. Click on any State/ Union Territory/ Region name, would further take you to the data table listing page, for the clicked State/Union Territory Region, under the said heading. Click the table heading to view the data table that will be shown in a separate window. In case you have not logged in, the following window would appear asking you to login. Once the data table is displayed, you may download the data table in MS-Excel, MS-Word or HTML format and/or print the table, subject to the rights assigned with your access. You can also search the data available on site thru the "Search" box shown prominently on the top of the site. The data available, relating to the keyword or phrase submitted by you would appear as search result in the form of a list of data table headings. You may click any table heading to view the data by following the instruction given in Para 'Navigating for Data' above.
