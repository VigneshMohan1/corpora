According to the 2011 Census, the total population of Himachal Pradesh is 6,856,509. In the previously 2001 Census, the population was 6,077,900. The male and female population from the entire population of Himachal Pradesh is 3,473,892 and 3,382,617 respectively. The total population growth since the last census was 12.81 percent and the population of the state forms 0.57 percent of the entire population in India. Himachal Pradesh has an area of 55,673 sq. km with a Density of 123 per sq km. The Sex Ratio in Himachal Pradesh is 974 for each 1000 male and has increased from 970 per 1000 males since the last census of 2001. The child sex ratio is 906 per 1000 males and has decreased as compared to 957 in the 2001 census. Himachal Pradesh has a Literacy rate of 83.78 percent, with male literacy at 90.83 percent and female literacy at 76.60 percent. In figures, the total literates in Himachal Pradesh are 5,104,506 out of which the males are 2,791,542 and females are 2,312,964.
