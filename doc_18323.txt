Goa is a popular national and international holiday destination and is loved by everyone. This is the only place in India where you can see foreigners enjoying months together at the white sands of Goa. Goa has a long history that is very interesting and was one of the richest ports of the Portuguese. Goa was ruled by the Portuguese for centuries and handed over to the Indian republic in 1961. This is a Union Territory of India and is located on the west coast of India in between the states of Maharashtra and Karnataka. Due to its renowned beaches and the world heritage architecture you can see thousands of tourists flocking to this place. The rich flora and fauna along with the biodiversity makes it the most popular destination for the whole family. Tourism is Goa's main economy, while the land is also rich in minerals and ores. Thus due to the large number of visitors for purposes of business and pleasure, Goa is full of hotels that will cater to the needs of all the visitors. You can all kinds of hotels all over Goa. Most of the hotels are lined up on the sea shore, while some are located in the city. Hotels here are world class and come in international style to cater to the large number of foreign tourists. Check out the resorts, or the cottages, 5 star hotels, 3 star hotels, and budget hotels that come with world-class amenities. Even the restaurants and eateries here are excellent. The food is something to be talked about. You can get international cuisine as well as local cuisine that is mouth watering. You can also check out stand-alone restaurants that produce a variety of dishes with fish and rice. List of Major Hotels: 1. Leela Palace, Goa 2. Taj Holiday Village, Goa 3.
