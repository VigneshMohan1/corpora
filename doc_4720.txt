Results Framework Document for R F D Department of Animal Husbandry, Dairying & Fisheries (2011-2012) Section 1: Vision, Mission, Objectives and Functions (2011-2012)Results Framework Document (RFD) for Department of Animal Husbandry, Dairying & Fisheries  Sustainable growth of livestock, poultry and fisheries for nutritional security and economic prosperity Mission  Preserve animal genetic resources, conserve indegienous breeds,  protect, strengthen and improve livestock and fish health, create employment opportunities, increase production, productivity and value addition of livestock, fish and poultry products. Objectives 1 Prevention and Control of animal diseases 2 Development of Fodder & feed 3 Increase fish production & provide assistance to fishermen 4 Development Of Poultry 5 Development of small ruminants 6 Genetic upgradation of live stock 7 Development & conservation of indigenous breeds 8 Increase milk production and provide assistance to farmers Functions Monitoring of animal health and control of animal diseases1 Delivery of breed improvement services2 Enhance production of livestock products3 Maintain census of livestock population4 The Department is responsible for matters relating to livestock production, preservation, protection and improvement of stocks, dairy development, fishing & fisheries. Advise the State Governments/Union Territories in the formulation of policies and programmes in the field of animal husbandry, dairy development and fisheries. The main focus of the activities is on: (a) Development of requisite infrastructure in States/UTs for improving animal productivity (b) Promoting infrastructure for handling, processing and marketing of milk and milk products. Planning Commission: Planning Commission have to give time-bound approval of EFC Memorandum for incorporating any changes in the schemes, increasing outlays etc. 2. Ministry of Finance: Ministry of Finance is involved in approving EFC for schemes with an outlay of more than Rs.150 Crores.  Ministry of Finace has to give time-bound approval of EFC. 3. State Governments: State Governments are implementing some programmes through various State Departments or are involved in transfer of funds to implementing agencies like Mlk Unions, Fish Farmer's Development Agency etc. If there is abnormal delay in transfer of funds the implementation of the project suffers. 4. NABARD:  NABARD is involved in implementing schems like Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Fund and Poultry Venture Capital Fund. If they do not get requisite project proposals from Banks then the scheme implementation suffers. 5.  Implementing Agencies: If Milk Unions, Livestock Development Boards, Fish Farmer's Development Agencies, Fodder Development Stations are not adequately staffed then the implementation suffers. page : 13 of 14 Section 6: OutCome/Impact of Department/Ministry (2011-2012)Results Framework Document (RFD) for Department of Animal Husbandry, Dairying & Fisheries OutCome/Impact of Department/Ministry Jointly responsible for influencing this outcome / impact with the following department (s) / ministry(ies) Success Indicator FY 09/10 FY 11/12FY 10/11 FY 12/13 FY 13/14 282 285Number of vaccinations donePrevention and control of animal diseases State Governments 285 2852851 112.5 118Production of Milk (in MMT)Increase in milk production State Governments, DoAC, Planning Commission 122 1261162 5.50 5.65Per Capita Consumption (in Kg/yr) Increase in per capita consumption of fish State Governments, MPEDA, DARE 5.70 5.805.603 page : 14 of 14
