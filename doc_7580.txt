These events include both indoor and outdoor events in which Asian countries can take part both at the team and individual level. The different types of sports in which players and athletes participate are as follows: Badminton, Baseball, Cross country running, Basketball, Futsal, Boxing, Judo, Figure skating, Karate, Handball, Rugby union, Swimming, Table tennis, Volleyball, Taekwondo, Tennis, Weightlifting, Wrestling, and Shooting. All these events are biennial events which take place after every two years.
