The company was successfully listed on the mainboard of Singapore Stock Exchange in year 2000, with many years of experience in Singapore, the company eventually expanded its expertise into Cambodia since year 2008 for property and agriculture business. PROPERTY DEVELOPMENT The company is focusing on the business of developing residential, commercial and industrial properties in Singapore and Cambodia. AGRICULTURE The company is focusing on all aspects of the business of agriculture and farm development.
