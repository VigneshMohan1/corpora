744-5339 Webmaster Login Info Oklahoma Crop Variety Trials Oklahoma Crop Variety Trials The Oklahoma Crop Variety Trials website brings together the results of all of the crop variety testing done each year by the OSU Division of Agricultural Sciences and Natural Resources . This information can be useful to producers in helping them make informed management decisions and variety choices each year. You will also find links to additional sites where you can find more in depth information about individual crops and the research being done on them. This site is the result of a collaborative effort from the Oklahoma Agricultural Experiment Station and the Oklahoma Cooperative Extension Service . For more information about your specific area, contact your local county Extension educator .
