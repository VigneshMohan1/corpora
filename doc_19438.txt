If you really want to feel like a king then nothing gets better than the Palace on Wheels travel package. Royal in the true sense of the term the Palace on Wheels is a regal journey to the land of the majestic Havelis, splendid forts and sand dunes. Apart from the dazzling state of Rajasthan the tour also includes a trip to the historical cities of Agra and Delhi . A luxurious train aptly named, the Palace on Wheels epitomizes the rich Indian heritage, hospitality and cuisine. A one of a life time experience the Palace on Wheels takes you through some of the most exotic regions in the world. Designed keeping in mind the absolute elite the Palace on Wheels is a train that is self sufficient. Centrally air conditioned, the luxury train comprises 14 deluxe saloons, modern amenities of everyday life, two dining cars decked up in true Rajasthani style and a host of attendants, khidmatgars, who are at your service 24X7. A rare moment of exuberance the Palace on Wheels provides you with an opportunity to relive the glorious past of the country. A seamless concoction of the ambience inside and the beauty outside could best be described by just one word, "magic". In case you want to be a part of this heart rendering trip then all you have to do is just finalize any Wednesday, since this moving castle starts every Wednesday evening at 1800 hrs from Delhi and ends on the following Wednesday early in the morning at 0730 hrs.
