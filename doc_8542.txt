Thanks to the hard work of our latest recruit, Ruth Muchaba, Access Agriculture is getting recognised for regular updates on Facebook, Twitter and soon to come WhatsApp.  Already there are more than 500 likes on Facebook from around Africa and the rest of the world.  With Twitter, you are able to keep up to date with the latest news on sustainable agriculture by connecting with @AccessAgric. The social media savvy can now test our beta version of Agtube at www.agtube.org . You just need to register, activate the account from the return e-mail and then upload short videos direct from your mobile, tablet or computer.  Social media for rural youth - Access Agriculture has it covered.
