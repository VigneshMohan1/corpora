This tournament of Twenty20 cricket is organised by the Board of Cricket for Control in India (BCCI), and follows the regulations of the IPL Governing Council. The Indian Premiere League 2015 is being headed by Ranjib Biswal, Chairman of the league. It has been a trend in the IPL that the city which bags the cup hosts the opening ceremony of the next series and the matches are held in different cities across the country. This year it is expected to be held in either Mumbai or Kolkata. If sources are to be believed, the opening ceremony may witness performances of Hollywood and Bollywood celebrities. Sponsorship: Pepsi will be the title sponsor of the eighth season of the league. Broadcasters: Sony SIX (English) and SET Max (Hindi) will be the official broadcasters of the tournament. Participating Teams in IPL 8 2015 Chennai Super Kings Delhi Daredevils Kings XI Punjab Kolkata Knight Riders Mumbai Indians Rajasthan Royals Royal Challengers Bangalore Sunrisers Hyderabad IPL History IPL began in 2008 and was founded by BCCI. Till 2012, IPL was sponsored by DLF, but in 2013, Pepsi took the charge of IPL. Pepsi paid an amount of about $72 million for a contract of five years.
