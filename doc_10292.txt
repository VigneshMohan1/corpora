Your shopping bag is empty. Go to the shop Bull Agro Implements Home Bull Agro Implements Manufactures and supplies farm equipments Bull Agro Implements was established in 1998 in Coimbatore. The major products offered by the company include rotary tiller machine, rotary tiller blades, and power tiller blades. Since its inception it has increased its production capacity. It currently manufactures 4 lakh blades in a month. The company holds an ISO 9001-2008 certificate for its strong sales network. The annual turnover of the company is Rs. 43 crores. Farmers from Karnataka, Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu contact Bull Agro for solutions. “Quality in spare parts, raw material, competitive price, and good service support are some of the unique features of our products,” shares Mr. R. Chandramohan, Managing Director. The manufacturing operations started with European technical know-how. Based on the requirements of the agriculture farmers, the company has spent three years to commercialize this technology with lot of Research and Development. Bull Agro is committed to achieve customer satisfaction by meeting the needs and expectations of the customer and is in continuous process of improvement of ISO 9000/ QMS in its process, system & products. The company’s products are made affordable for farmers. Farmers can avail Government subsidy to purchase Bull Agro’s products and can approach its authorized dealers for the same. The company creates awareness of its products through agri event, pamphlets, flexi board in rural areas and display boards in dealers’ shops. The company has good markets in Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Maharashtra, Gujarat, Orissa and West Bengal. They also plan to capture Haryana, Punjab, Uttar Pradesh and Bihar. “Through our authorized dealers we offer after sale services to buyers,” Mr. Chandra Mohan adds. Mr. Chandramohan is a management graduate who joined Bull Agro Implements in 1998. He holds more than 15 years’ experience in manufacturing and marketing.
