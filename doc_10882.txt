Go to the shop Rainbow Agro Enterprises – Contract farming Home Rainbow Agro Enterprises – Contract farming Involved in providing contract service since 2003 Rainbow Agro Enterprises was established in the year 1999. The annual turnover of the company ranges from Rs. 2.5 to 4 crores. Located at Chittor, Andhra Pradesh. Mr. Ramesh Jain believes, contract farming will be rolling the Indian economy in the future. A farmer can save their yield through contract farming. Although contract farming must first and foremost be considered as a commercial proposition, it has also come to be viewed as an effective approach to help solve many of the market access and input supply problems faced by small farmers. Rainbow Agro has been associated with contract farming since 2003. The company raises awareness through meeting with farmers. It currently works in Andhra Pradesh and plans to expand its business in Bangalore and other nearby cities. The joining procedure of the company is simple. Crop potential, crop involvement and government subsidies are some of the pre requisites that the company looks out for. Farmers willing to associate themselves with Rainbow Agro Enterprises, through contract farming may send their request needs via email to rainbowagrocorporation@gmail.com.
