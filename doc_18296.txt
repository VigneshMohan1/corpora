Tirupati, one of the wealthiest holy cities in the world, is situated in the district of Chittoor, Andhra Pradesh. One of the most popular temples in religious lore, the Venkateswara Swamy temple or Tirupati Balaji in Tirupati pulls in millions of worshippers from across the world every year. To accommodate the pilgrims, several ashrams and dharamshalas, have been set up by the government as well as temple authorities in the city in the vicinity of the temple. Moreover, for those who cannot afford to pay the rent, the Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams has established Public Amenities Complexes to provide dormitory facility to the devotees. The city outpouring divine grace receives maximum devotees during the days of Hindu festivals, which are celebrated in a grand way in the temples of the city. So, make sure you pre-book your accommodation if you happen to visit the city in the peak season.
