Plans to cultivate and supply selected anti-cancer herbs Alsmedherbs, also known as Ancient Herbs, was established 10 years ago. Mr. S. M. Shahul Hamid, who holds a B. Pharma and has 35 years of experience in the field, is the founder. The turnover of the company last year was around Rs. 3.5 Crores. The company deals with about 45 types of herbs. “But we choose only against specific inquiry from our buyers such as Coleous, Turmeric, Glorisa Superba seeds,” shares Mr. Shahul. The company has been cultivating herbs through contracts with farmers since the last 15 years. Co-operative method to understand rates of herbs to be paid to farmers is adopted. Manufacturers of herbal food supplements are the target customers. Herbs and seeds are supplied to Indian Herbal Extraction units. Some are also exported on buyer’s request. New markets and demands are identified through the web and contacts in the field. “By assuring best quality with competitive rate and supply in time, we create awareness our products among our customers,” Mr. Shahul adds. Since organic certified products enjoy better rates in overseas market, the company supplies organic certified products to Australia. They believe in understanding buyer’s needs and fulfilling the same. About 60% of Allopathic drugs are derived from herbal sources. Alsmedherbs is planning big to cultivate and outsource certain anti-cancer herbs for their overseas clients. Plans to develop an agri cooperative society for each herb and supplying to International market is also on the cards. Mr. S. M. Shahul Hamid started his career as a Medical Representative in 1973. He then moved on to be a manufacturing chemist in a company in Madurai. He established Alsmedherbs Pvt Ltd in 1990’s and has been continuing his successful stint in the company since then.
